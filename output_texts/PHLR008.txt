PURPOSE: 
To provide the accurate receipt of physician orders called into the department for collection 
Addresses: 
Venipuncture Charge Techs 
Venipuncture Phlebotomists 
 
POLICY: 
A. Laboratory testing of patient samples may not be performed without a physician order 
B. Physicians or their representatives may call in orders for patients to have laboratory testing performed. 
C. Because of the complexity of understanding testing being requested and the requirements of documenting 
the received order, Venipuncture personnel are not to accept verbal or called in orders for laboratory 
testing. 
D. Requests of this nature are to be referred to the Hematology Charge Tech who can be reached in the 
Hematology Department or by calling ext. 5-8291 
 
PROCEDURE: 
1. Phone calls or verbal requests received requesting patient testing are to be transferred to the Hematology 
Charge Tech 
2. When receiving a request, listen to the requestor 
3. Politely explain that the requests for testing are accepted by the Hematology Department 
4. Transfer the call or direct the person making the request to the Hematology Department.
