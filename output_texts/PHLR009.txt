PURPOSE: 
Provide a means of meeting phlebotomy service demands of Outreach clients after normal business hours 
 
Addresses: 
Venipuncture Charge Techs 
Venipuncture Phlebotomists 
 
POLICY: 
A. The Outreach section of the Laboratory provides an “on call” phlebotomist to draw after hour phlebotomy 
requests 
B. The Venipuncture Charge Tech is given a daily/weekend outreach “on call” list that includes the name, hours 
of service, and phone number of the person assigned to after hour blood draws 
C. When an Outreach client calls to request service, the Venipuncture Charge Tech is to call the appropriate “on 
call” phlebotomist and report the organization name, request priority, and the name and number of person 
requesting the service 
D. If unable to contact the “on call” phlebotomist, the Venipuncture Charge Tech is to call the Outreach 
Supervisor responsible for the specimen collection services (listed on the outreach “on-call list”/the 
Hematology Charge Tech will have the required number)
