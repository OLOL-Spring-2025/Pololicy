PURPOSE: 
To establish policy for the ratio for clinical supervision of Student Certified Registered Nurse 
Anesthetists (CRNA) by an Anesthesiologist or CRNA. 
 
POLICY: 
Student CRNA’s must be supervised during their clinical rotation assignments by a CRNA or 
Anesthesiologist.  The student CRNA will be supervised on a 1:1 ratio or 1:2 ratio depending on 
the program level of the student.  The CRNA or Anesthesiologist must be immediately available to 
the Student CRNA at all times when actual clinical anesthesia care is being administered however, 
CRNA or Anesthesiologist must be present during induction or extubation.
