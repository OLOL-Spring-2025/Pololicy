Distribution:         Cardiovascular Lab/Critical Care/Cardiology Units 
 
Formulated by:        Cardiology Services/Critical Care Services 
 
Purpose:            To provide a policy for injection of subcutaneous local 
                       anesthesia for patient comfort during femoral sheath 
                       removal. 
   
Applies to:           Cardiovascular Lab Personnel/Critical Care RN's/Cardiology Units 
 
Policy:     Designated personnel should follow established procedure for 
                       administration of subcutaneous local anesthesia. 
 
Reference:            LA. Nurse Practice Act - Section 9-30, Subsection D. 
 
Procedure:  
 
1. After obtaining a physician order, sheath site should be examined prior to administration of 
local anesthesia. 
2. Refer to facility designated on-line clinical reference.
