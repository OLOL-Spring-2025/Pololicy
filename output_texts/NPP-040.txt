PURPOSE:  
To provide guidelines for the nursing staff as it relates to flavoring medication in the pediatric patient within the 
Children’s Hospital. 
 
POLICY: 
 
I. FLAVORx 
A. Each unit will maintain a variety of FLAVORx as needed for patient population. 
B. FLAVORx mixing will be in accordance with manufactures guidelines based on medications 
requiring flavoring. 
C. Manufacture expiration date on individual flavoring bottles will be used as the shelf life of opened 
bottles.  Note FLAVORx states the shelf-life of the flavorings is eight years. 
 
II. Syrpalta or other Simple Syrup Flavoring 
A. Each unit will maintain multi-dose bottle if Syrpalta and/or other simple syrup flavoring as available. 
B. Flavoring will be used in medications based on the needs of the patient and the nurse’s discretion.  
C. Manufacture expiration date on flavoring container will be used as the shelf-life of open bottles.
