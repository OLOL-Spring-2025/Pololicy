PURPOSE: 
 
To ensure effective communication to the Medical Staff and Advanced Practice Professionals. 
 
POLICY: 
 
The Medical Staff Office will distribute appropriate information to the Medical Staff and 
Advanced Practice Professionals. 
 
PROCEDURES: 
 
1. The Medical Staff Office shall distribute information to the Medical Staff and Advanced 
Practice Professionals as appropriate. Distribution to the Medical Staff  and Advanced 
Practice Professionals may be through interoffice/in -house mail, posted mail, facsimile,  
electronic mail, text message, or posted on LakeMD. 
 
2. Information to be distributed by the Medical Staff Office includes, but is not limited to: 
• Bylaws, Rules and Regulations, Policies/Procedures 
• Medical Staff Newsletter 
• Informational notices 
• Meeting Notices/Cancellations 
• Items specified by the Chief Medical Officer , Chief of Staff, or Chief Executive 
Officer 
 
3. Exceptions to the above may be indicated by the Chief Executive Officer, Chief Medical 
Officer, Associate Chief Medical Officer, and/or Chief of Staff.
