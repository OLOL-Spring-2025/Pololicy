PURPOSE: 
 
To establish guidelines for the correct registration of all Executive Wellness Patients. 
 
 
PROCEDURE: 
 
Executive Wellness Patients are scheduled by the Site Coordinator of Executive Wellness 
Program.  The information is sent to Patient Access for pre-registration via email on a 
weekly basis.  A pre-registration is completed with patient’s demographics, “Self-Pay - 
Admitter should select "DO NOT BILL INSURANCE" on the coverage screen;  
Admitter will remove insurance from the HAR.  (Do NOT delete insurance coverage on 
Detailed View.); the guarantor is “Patient”. 
 
Patient will then be bought to the Medical office Plaza to be (3) ID and arm banded. After 
all test are complete, the patient will be escorted by clinical staff to St. Mary’s 
Admissions Unit to be admitted. St. Mary’s Admissions Clinical Staff will notify Patient 
Access team of patients’ arrival and location. Patient Access team will visit (check in) 
patient, Clinical Service Representative is responsible for preparing chart.
