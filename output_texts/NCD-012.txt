Purpose:              To provide instructions for room clean up in 
                    Cardiovascular Lab. 
 
Applies to:          Cardiovascular Lab Staff, Housekeeping 
 
Policy: 
 
1. Procedure rooms shall be cleaned upon the completion of a procedure after the patient is 
removed from the procedure area. 
a. The room shall be mopped with a cleansing solution and floor cleaned. 
b. The table shall be cleaned with Sani-cloth wipes or a Virex solution allowing for 
appropriate “wet time” and a sheet applied to table mat. 
c. Trash will be removed and will be replaced with new trash bags. 
d. Any visible blood shall be wiped from the area with a virex solution and then 
cleaned with Sani-cloth. 
e. Room will be ready for the next patient. 
f. All supplies need to placed in appropriate place prior to leaving for the day. 
 
2. Housekeeping should terminally clean rooms after cases are completed each day. A 
microfiber pad and cleaning solution is available in each room for cleaning between 
cases.  
 
3. Equipment should be cleaned before it is brought into the procedure room.
