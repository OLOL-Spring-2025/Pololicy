PURPOSE 
The purpose of this policy is to describe the process for filling vacant positions as they occur at Our 
Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center (OLOL). 
 
POLICY 
1. Formal approval is required to fill all vacant positions at OLOL, whether or not the position has been 
previously authorized or budgeted.  
2. A Manager seeking to fill such a vacancy must use the online position requisition process and obtain 
the necessary approvals.   
3. A Position Requisition Committee meets regularly to approve or deny all requests to fill positions.
