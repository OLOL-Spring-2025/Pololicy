Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center  Chapter:  VIII 
Subject: Coroner’s Request for Medical Information  
Date of Origination:  October 2015   Supersedes:   
Date of Last Revision:  September 2019 
Date of Last Review: May 2021 Serial Code:  HIMR37 
 
 
Purpose:  To define the proper procedure for a coroner requesting medical information. 
 
 
Policy:  It is the policy of Our Lady of the Lake RMC HIM Department to fulfill a request issued by 
the Coroner’s Office. 
   
Procedure: 
 
1. Once a request is made by the Coroner to obtain records, the HIM/Release of information 
department is contacted of the request.  These requests are honored without consent 
requirements.  See Policy HIMR22-Release of Information. 
 
 
2. Between the hours of 8:00AM to 4:30PM, the Release of Information staff will process the 
request for medical records.  If the request occurs after 4:30PM on weekdays or on weekends, 
the HIM staff or the ER Service team will release the requested medical records to the Coroner.   
 
• HIM staff shall follow the above process for release between 4:30PM and 6 PM 
(Monday to Friday) and 8:00AM to 4:30PM (Saturday and Sunday). 
• ER Service staff will process record requests between the hours of 6:00PM and 
6:00AM (Monday to Friday) and 4:30PM to 8:00AM (Saturday and Sunday). 
• House Manager will process Inpatient Unit record requests between the hours of 
6:00PM and 6:00AM (Monday to Friday) and 4:30PM to 8:00AM (Saturday and 
Sunday).
