PURPOSE: 
To allow for the redrawing of patients already armbanded with the Blood Bank Identification System 
 
Addresses: 
Supervisors 
Venipuncture Charge Techs 
Venipuncture Phlebotomists 
 
POLICY: 
Patients may be redrawn at Transfusion Services personnel request for several reasons.  Occasionally additional 
sample is required to complete a patient blood bank work-up. Patients having a reaction while receiving blood are 
redrawn to establish that correct procedures for cross-matching have been followed. 
  
A. Transfusion Services personnel will inform the Venipuncture Charge Tech of the need for redrawing 
B. The reason and urgency of the request is made known 
C. A Phlebotomist is dispatched to redraw the patient 
D. The Phlebotomist performing the redraw is to carefully: 
1. Identify the patient 
2. Draw a 9 ml purple top tube 
3. Label the tube with 
❖ Patient name, first and last 
❖ Patient date of birth  
❖ Date and time of draw 
❖ Network ID code of person drawing 
❖ Write or place the label of the Blood Bank Identification number on the tube that  
 matches the current Blood Bank Identification number on the Blood Bank  
 Armband located on the patient’s wrist or ankle. 
E. The redrawn sample is quickly returned to the laboratory for testing 
F. If the patient does not have a Blood Bank Identification Armband on immediately contact the Venipuncture 
Charge Tech and/or Transfusion Services before drawing the patient.
