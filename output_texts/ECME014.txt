Policy 
 
Qualified hospital staff inspect, test and calibrate nuclear medicine equipment annually. The dates of these activities 
are documented. 
 
Annual inspection, testing and calibration of nuclear medicine equipment is performed jointly by manufacturer 
service personnel, Biomedical Services personnel and RND Medical Physics. Documentation of annual testing and 
calibration is maintained in the Radiology department and reviewed by the Radiation Safety Officer.
