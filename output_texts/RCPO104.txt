POLICY: 
 Medical direction of the Physical Medicine/Rehabilitation department is provided by the facility’s medical 
director who is knowledgeable regarding the scope of practice for Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy, and 
Speech-Language pathology.  The care of every patient is under the general medical direction of a physician. 
 
Administrative direction of the Physical Medicine/Rehab program is provided by the Department’s 
Administrative Director.
