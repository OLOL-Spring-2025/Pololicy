PURPOSE: 
To define the process for cleaning equipment and toys in the Children’s Hospital. 
 
POLICY: 
The following guidelines will be used to clean equipment and toys in the Children’s Hospital. 
 
1. A hospital approved germicidal wipe is used to clean toys.  All toys are cleaned upon return to the 
Playroom from a patient’s room.  Stationary items in the Playroom are cleaned prior to the opening of 
the Playroom, and as needed.   
 
2. Any item returned from a patient’s room in isolation for C-diff will be cleaned using the hospital 
approved germicidal wipes with bleach. 
 
3. Approved spray disinfectant will be used to clean items such as books, games and other items where 
approved germicidal wipes cannot be used. 
 
4. Toys will be cleaned in between patient use.  Toys brought to a patient’s room will be returned to the 
dirty toy bin for cleaning.  Toys in the playroom will be returned to the dirty toy bin after patient use to 
be cleaned. 
 
5. Toys which are able to be placed in the dishwasher are sanitized. 
 
6. Utility carts and wagons are cleaned with the hospital approved germicidal wipe and are sprayed with a 
disinfectant spray in-between uses and labeled “clean” for next patient use.
