POLICIES AND PROCEDURES MANUAL Manual:  Rehabilitation Center 
Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center Chapter:  Documentation 
Subject:  Therapy Discharge Summary Section:  500 – Discharge 
Summary 
Date of Origination:  October 1989 Supersedes:  
Date Last Reviewed: July 4, 2024 Serial Code:  RC/D/501 
  
PURPOSE: To devise an efficient, but comprehensive system to ensure that discharge documentation 
requirements are fulfilled, and follow-up services needed for continuity of care is provided. 
 
POLICY: Discharge summary documents will be completed by each discipline so that they can be sent to 
the respective follow-up service (home health agency, OP clinic, etc.) on the day of discharge. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
 
 
1. Discharge summaries are completed by the therapist upon completion of treatment.  Therapy may be 
discontinued by physician order prior to the patient’s discharge from the hospital. 
 
2. Discharge documentation should include assessment of patient’s status at point of discharge, and any 
additional service patients may need post-discharge. 
 
3. Discharge summaries must be written after the final therapy session has ended. Discharge summaries must 
include the reason for discontinuation of care, the degree of goal achievement and a discharge plan.
