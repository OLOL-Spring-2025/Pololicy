POLICY: The Rehabilitation Physician is responsible for coordinating medical and rehabilitation efforts in 
the care of patients in the Rehabilitation center.  Therefore, the Rehabilitation Physician must be 
aware of all medical and rehabilitative changes and conditions affecting patients at the 
Rehabilitation Center.  The Rehabilitation Physician should coordinate all services provided to 
the patient and is responsible for assuring that services provided are complimentary and not 
contradictory.  The Rehabilitation Physician will conduct patient visits at a minimum frequency 
of 3 face-to-face visits each week.   
 
PROCEDURE: 
 
When issues of concern arise regarding medical and/or rehabilitative care of patients at the 
Rehabilitation Center, the Rehabilitation Physician shall be appraised by written notation or telephone 
call.  The Rehabilitation Physician will then make appropriate decisions for the satisfaction of the 
situation or contact the appropriate consultant or rehabilitation professional to make sure that the 
situation is handled appropriately.  In some instances, the Rehabilitation Physician may direct the nurse 
or the other professional to contact an appropriate consultant by informing that professional as to the 
issues to be raided and the questions to be answered.
