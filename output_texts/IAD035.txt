PURPOSE: 
To provide guidelines for perfoming Imaging procedures in order to obtain 
diagnostic quality studies. 
POLICY: 
1. Proper orders will be obtained to perform imaging study. 
2. All procedures will be performed utilizing appropriate radiation protection. (ALARA) 
3. Lab results will be reviewed by technologist for any procedure requiring contrast. 
4. Technologist will properly 2ID/3ID patient before beginning procedure. 
5. All procedures will be completed according to protocols established and approved by 
Radiology Medical Director and in conjuction with manufactures recommended guidelines. 
REFERENCE: 
 Updated “Protocol Book” will be maintained On-line or in Department.
