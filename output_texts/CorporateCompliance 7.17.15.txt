PURPOSE: 
 
To comply with all laws and with the highest ethical standards throughout the organization. 
 
POLICY: 
 
All Access Services Staff have an obligation to report any concern or possible violation of 
Federal and State laws and regulations or ethical standards. 
 
PROCEDURES: 
 
Access Services Staff may report any concerns to their proper supervisor, someone in their chain 
of command or the Compliance Hotline at (225)765-6507 or you can call our toll free hotline 
(888)-400-4517.
