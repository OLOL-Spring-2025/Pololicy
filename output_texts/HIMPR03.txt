PURPOSE: 
To provide a means for clearing physician deficiencies for untimely deaths and for physicians who no 
longer practice at Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center. 
 
POLICY: 
It is our policy to reassign and complete physician deficiencies when a physician has expired or is no 
longer practicing at this facility. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
1. Identify deceased and/or inactive physicians. 
2. Print out the deficiency list for the physician. 
3. Present the deficiency listing to the Chief Medical Officer or designee and obtain approval for 
the administrative closure of records. 
4. Once approved, the HIM designee will reassign the outstanding deficiencies from these charts to 
Admin Override. 
5. All documentation of administrative closures of records will be kept on file in the HIM 
Department.
