PURPOSE: 
To provide an interdisciplinary system of communication among all staff for efficient and effective patient care. 
 
POLICY: 
Rehabilitation Staff will use the following methods of communication to express patient needs to all treatment 
team members. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
1. FACT Cards (see separate policy) 
a. Information cards that hang on the patient’s wheelchair 
b. Information includes levels of assistance and special precautions among various disciplines 
c. Cards are to be updated weekly and all entries must be dated. 
2. Schedule Board 
a. The main schedule board is located in the Rehabilitation gym  
b. The patient’s therapy schedule will be posted in each patient’s room by a Physical Therapy Aide. 
c. The patient’s schedule may consist, but is not limited to Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy, 
Speech Therapy and Activity Therapy. 
d. Schedule changes will be updated on the Rehab gym scheduling board and patient room at the end of 
the day. 
3. Morning Report 
a. Completed each morning by nursing staff to denote changes in patient’s status from the previous 
evening 
b. A representative from the therapy department will collect the report from the charge nurse each 
morning and deliver necessary messages to the primary therapists and /or make notations on the rehab 
gym scheduling board for the primary therapists to review. 
4. Team Conferences (see separate policy) 
a. Weekly team conferences will be held to discuss patient concerns, current status, treatment 
progression, goal status, and discharge planning 
Informal meetings may be conducted by case management as needed.
