PURPOSE: 
   
POLICY: 
 All employees receive orientation to the Physical Medicine/Rehabilitation department upon initial employment. 
PROCEDURE: 
 
1. The department leader will ensure that all new employees are scheduled to receive departmental orientation 
upon initial employment. 
2. The Supervisor or other designated staff persons will review all topics addressed on the orientation checklist 
with the new employee during the employee’s orientation period (usually within first 2-4 weeks of 
employment). 
3. Each employee should be oriented to the department as well as all disciplines housed within the department 
during this period. 
4. Once orientation is complete, all checklists must be signed and dated by the new employee and the orientation 
preceptor and given to the Supervisor to keep in the employee folder/ electronic filing system.
