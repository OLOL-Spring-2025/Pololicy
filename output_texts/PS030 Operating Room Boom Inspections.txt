Title: Operating Room Boom Inspections  
Revision Date:  
Review Date: 09/30/2021 
Reference # PS030 Origin Date: January 2018 
 
POLICY: 
To provide a safe physical environment for patients, staff and visitors at all Our Lady of the Lake 
Regional Medical Center (OLOL) network locations. 
 
PROCEDURES: 
OLOL’s operating room boom systems have been installed and tested in accordance with current 
standards and manufacturer’s recommendations.   
 
Inspection requirements include each boom be leak tested and hoses be visually inspected at least every 
18 months. 
 
REFERENCES: 
National Fire Protection Agency (NFPA) 99, 2012 Edition, Health Care Facilities Code Handbook, 
Section 5.1.14.2.3.2
