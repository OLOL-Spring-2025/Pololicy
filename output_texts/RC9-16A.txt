PURPOSE: 
To identify which blood gas machine analyzed a specific patient’s blood gas results 
 
PROCEDURES 
A. Access RALSInformation System.  
B. Select Blood Tests with appropriate date range.   
C. Change the date range if further back than 30 days as necessary.   
D. Select the Patient ID. 
E. The host and reader ID will be identified with the Patient ID selection.
