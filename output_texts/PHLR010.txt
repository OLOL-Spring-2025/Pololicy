PHLR010 
EVACUATION OF HANDICAPPED PERSONS 
       Date Issue Date Effective Date 
 Prepared/Revised By ZJA/LC 5/4/1995 5/4/1995 5/4/1995 
Reviewed By LC 9/7/2019 Origination Date 
Medical Director Review A Harton 9/22/2019 APR 30, 1995 
 
PURPOSE: 
To define actions taken in the evacuation of handicapped patients, guests, or visitors during the event of an 
emergency 
 
POLICY: 
The Phlebotomy Charge Technician(s) and the Blood Donor In-house Team Leader are responsible for assigning 
personnel to assist in evacuating handicapped persons from the Waiting Room, Outpatient Drawing Station, Exam 
Room, Bone Marrow Room and the Blood Donor area in an emergency.  Section supervisory personnel will be 
responsible for the evacuation of handicapped persons from their sections. 
 
A handicapped person will be evacuated according to the evacuation routes posted in each of the above locations. 
 
Individuals unable to walk may be transported by wheelchair, in the bed located in the Bone Marrow Room, or by 
placing the person on a blanket and pulling them to safety.
