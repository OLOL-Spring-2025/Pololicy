PURPOSE: 
To define the governance structure for nursing at Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center. 
 
POLICY:  
 
Nurse Executive Council (NEC) Function 
The Council is the central decision-making body for the practice of nursing   within the Baton Rouge 
Market of the Franciscan Missionaries of Our Lady Health System (FMOLHS) which includes: Our Lady of the 
Lake Regional Medical Center, Our Lady of the Lake Ascension, Our Lady of the Lake Childrenâ€™s Hospital, Our 
Lady of the Lake Livingston, Our Lady of the Lake North Baton Rouge and Our Lady of the Lake Physician Group 
(FMOLHS BR Market)   
 
A.  The Council advises, facilitates, and assists in the coordination of nursing operations and 
activities. 
 
B.  The Nurse Executive Council (NEC) has decision and veto power in regard to professional 
nursing practice and nursing operations. 
 
C. All nursing committees report up through NEC. 
 
D. Refer to Organization Shared Governance structure
