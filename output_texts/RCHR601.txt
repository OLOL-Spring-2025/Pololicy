POLICY: Student affiliations are established with universities that have PT, OT, ST programs approved by 
the appropriate national therapy association. 
PROCEDURE: 
 
1. Each student is assigned to a clinical supervisor who is responsible for supervising the student at all times. 
2. Each student affiliating in the Physical Medicine/Rehab division will be responsible for following the policies 
and procedures of the department and the hospital. 
3. A current written contract is on file through the Academic office for each affiliated university that utilizes our 
facility for clinical education purposes. 
4. A copy of the final evaluation shall be kept on file for one year. 
5. Students are not permitted to use the Clinical Information System until they attend a mandatory class, pass a 
test to demonstrate proficiency with regard to patient documentation.  At this time Epic access will be granted 
to the student and log in information will be given. 
6. Our Lady of the Lake RMC does not discriminate against students on the basis of sex, race, color, religion or 
national origin. 
7. All absences must be made up at the discretion of the CCCE in accordance with university policy.
