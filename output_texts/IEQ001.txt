Purpose: 
 
To establish guidelines pertaining to the preventative maintenance of imaging 
equipment. 
 
Policy: 
â€¢ Preventative Maintenance 
 
 
A. It is the responsibility of the Biomedical Services department to ensure all 
equipment undergoes preventative maintenance in accordance with the 
schedule set forth in Biomedical Services in an effort to : 
i. Identify potential operational problems before equipment 
breakdown 
ii. Contribute to the continued safety of patients and employees of 
the Imaging department 
iii. Contribute to efforts in Risk Management through equipment 
maintenance. 
iv. Meet manufactures recommended guidelines and requirements 
of regulatory agencies. 
v. Evaluate all patient related equipment prior to lease or 
purchase to assure procurement of safe, low maintenance 
equipment. 
vi. Assure proper execution of service contracts, thereby 
contributing to cost containment efforts. 
B. Biomed and Imaging department will work together to establish routine 
schedule to accomplish any preventative maintenance needed.  
C. All preventative maintenance records either performed by Biomed or outside 
contracted vendors will be kept by the Biomedical Services department.
