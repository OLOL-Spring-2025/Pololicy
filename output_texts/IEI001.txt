PURPOSE: 
To insure every employee is oriented regarding services provided in the Imaging division and 
provide education regarding the operations and the regulatory requirements.  The purpose of 
the Initial Orientation and Competency Checklist is to have a record of the completed 
employee orientation process. 
 
POLICY:  
A. Licensed personnel must demonstrate competency regarding knowledge and use of correct 
operating procedures for equipment. 
 
B. At the end of the orientation period, the employee and preceptor should sign off on the 
demonstrated competencies. 
 
C. Initial Orientation and Competency Checklist: 
1. To be completed within 90 days of hire date. 
2. To be initialed and dated in each area by instructing individual. 
3. To be reviewed with new employee and supervisor at the end of the orientation period. 
4. Provisions to be made for areas not covered due to equipment failure, illness, etc. 
5. The completed orientation form to be filed in the department's employee file. 
6. Upon successful completion employee shall have proven competency in all areas of the 
modality of which they are working/assigned. 
 
D. Orientation may be extended for an additional 90 days at the discretion of the Imaging 
Services manager.
