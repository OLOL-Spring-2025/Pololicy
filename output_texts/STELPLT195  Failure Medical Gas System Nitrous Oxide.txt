Policy: 
  
In the event of a malfunction and/or failure of the nitrous oxide system, the following 
procedure will be followed. 
  
Responsibility: 
  
Plant Operations 
  
Procedure: 
  
1.                  Notify the Manager of Facilities and/or Supervisor/designee, Administrative 
              Supervisor, and the departments that use nitrous oxide (i.e. Operating Room). 
  
2.                  Check to see that the manifold valve and regulator are properly aligned on the   
              nitrous oxide bulk supply tank. 
  
3.                  Replace empty tanks as necessary. 
  
4.                  If the tanks are not empty and the alignment is correct, check for point of   
             disruption in the system.  If the Plant Operations department personnel are unable   
             to correct the problem, request outside assistance from Preventive       
             Maintenance for Gulf Coast (Billy Turner) at 225-806-0785 
  
5.         Request emergency delivery of nitrous oxide cylinders from Air Gas, Account 
#55081 @ 1-800-323-1970.
