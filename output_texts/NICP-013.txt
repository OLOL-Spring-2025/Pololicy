PURPOSE: 
To state the procedure for closing NICU when census is zero. 
 
POLICY: 
When the census drops to 0, the charge nurse will: 
1. Notify house supervisor of closure. 
2. Assure all rooms are stocked and cleaned. 
3. Place all equipment and supply carts in clean equipment room, crash cart moved into medication room. 
4. Transfer all phone lines to PICU. 
5. Notify PBX operator of closure and that the phones have been transferred. 
6. Turn lights off or on low. 
7. Charge nurse continues to complete charge nurse check list each shift, i.e., checks logs, crash cart, etc. 
8. When NICU is closed, RN will remain in house and should be assigned to perform duties at the 
direction of the House Supervisor.
