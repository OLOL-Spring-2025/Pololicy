SCOPE: 
Applies to Franciscan Missionaries of Our Lady Health System, Inc. (FMOLHS) and its wholly owned sponsored 
organizations that propose and adopt System wide policies. 
PURPOSE: 
To ensure that cancer patients have access to rehabilitation services in order to restore optimal function and quality of 
life to people with physical impairment or disability resulting from their cancer or treatments for cancer.    
POLICY: 
The patients care team will collaborate with the Rehabilitation team to perform an assessment and provide appropriate 
referral for rehabilitation services.    
DEFINITIONS: 
N/A  
PROCEDURE: 
I. Rehabilitation services are provided to cancer patient s by physician, NP, or PA referral for both inpatient and 
outpatient settings. 
II. Rehabilitation services include: 
III. Inpatient:  occupational therapy, physical therapy, and speech therapy 
IV. Outpatient: occupational therapy, physical therapy, speech therapy

VI. Formal referrals are made by the physician, NP, or PA by placing an order in Epic. 
VII. Criteria for performing functional assessment:  see the following policies : ACAD101, ACAD201, ACIS102, 
ACIS202, ACIS207, ACPO201. 
VIII. Each calendar year, the cancer committee will monitor, evaluate, and make recommendations for improvements 
to rehabilitation care services.
