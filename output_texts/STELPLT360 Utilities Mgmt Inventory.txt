Policy: 
  
A current inventory of all equipment included in the Utilities Management Program will 
be accurate on a continuing basis and shall be recorded within one month of change, 
addition or deletion.   
  
The inventory of utilities equipment is located in the office of the Plant Operations 
Supervisor in the computer with backup. Hardcopy is over the desk in Utilities 
Management Manual. 
  
This inventory is also contained in the Preventative Maintenance file located in the Plant 
Operations office. 
  
The utilities inventory must be kept separate from other utilities information and must be 
produced as a separate entity.  The inventory must include all equipment associated with 
these systems.
