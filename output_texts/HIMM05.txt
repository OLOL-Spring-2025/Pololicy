PURPOSE:  
 
OLOL is committed to assessing and correcting as needed the documentation and timely completion of its 
medical records. 
 
 To establish an ongoing planned and systematic approach to reviewing records, and aggregating data to 
identify opportunities for improvement; taking action as needed and conducting follow-up. 
 
POLICY:        
 
The ongoing records review program will include inpatient and outpatient records and clinical 
services within the organization. 
 
TOPICS: 
Topics include, but are not limited to, the items required by the TJC. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
 
A. A designated HIM employee will complete a medical record review for all units. 
The review will consist of random sample 6-10 medical records dependent on unit 
size and/or census per unit per month. A complete and detailed review form will be 
used for each record to ensure that the entire record is evaluated. 
B. Once the reviews are completed, the data is tallied for final results. 
C. All results will be sent to Regulatory Management, and Nursing Services for 
review
