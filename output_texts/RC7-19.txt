I. Purpose: The purpose of this policy is to outline the process for respiratory therapists 
to administer care under the Adult Bronchodilator Assessment protocol. This adult 
bronchodilator protocol applies solely to the adult population outside of the ICU. This 
protocol has been reviewed and approved by the Respiratory Informatics Committee, 
Respiratory PAC, and the Medical Executive Committee 
 
II. Protocol: After having demonstrated competence, licensed Respiratory Therapists 
shall be authorized to administer care under this protocol when ordered by a 
physician or authorized provider. The physician may opt out of this protocol by 
indicating “no protocol” in the order.  See FMOLHS Adult Bronchodilator Protocol 
 
 
This policy is being archived.  Please refer to the policy RC12-5 for implementation and 
list of protocols.
