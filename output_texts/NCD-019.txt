Purpose:              To provide direction for a safe storage of gases in 
                       compressed cylinder tanks. 
   
Applies to:           CVL Personnel 
 
Policy: 
 
All Full/Partial gases in compressed cylinders are stored in the Cardiovascular Lab 
equipment room in a secure upright storage rack. Full and Partial gas cylinders are stored in 
separate racks and labeled appropriately.  Empty cylinders are to be stored in the soiled utility room 
and labeled as empty.  Materials Management department will obtain cylinders from soiled utility 
for replacements.
