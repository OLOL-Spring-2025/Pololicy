PURPOSE: 
To provide guidelines for the patient orientation process. 
 
POLICY: 
Staff will insure timely and thorough orientation of each patient admitted. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
The orientation process will include, but not necessarily limited to: 
1. Upon admission, the patient will be oriented to the unit by the assigned staff member. 
2. A review of the written Unit Guidelines and documentation by staff that review has been 
conducted. 
3. Location of the daily schedule, the Mental Health Advocate phone number and the Patient Rights 
, and/or patient handbook/pamphlet have been reviewed. 
4. The staff will conduct orientation for each patient within twelve (12) hours of admission but no 
later than twenty-four (24) hours. 
5. Orientation will be documented in the patient’s medical record. 
6. All staff will be responsible for responding to patient’s questions in a prompt, knowledgeable 
and respectful manner.
