POLICY:  
Nursing team members can request a Social Worker to meet with patients and/or family members for 
reasons including but not limited to: 
1. Discharge planning and care coordination  
2. Psychosocial assessment and referral to community resources. 
3. Assist patients and families with personal and environmental difficulties that may pose barriers 
to accessing care or optimal outcomes. 
4. Supportive counseling, crisis intervention, and connection to mental health services.   
5. Other identified psychosocial needs 
 
PROCEDURES:   
A. Referral can be made by: 
1. Discussion in Interdisciplinary Team Huddle (IDT) 
2. Voalte messaging  
3. Calling the Social Worker on assigned unit directly or calling the Care Management 
Department at 225-765-8892.  
a. OLOLA please call the Social Service Department at 225-621-2947. 
4. Entering a consult for Medical Management in the EMR.
