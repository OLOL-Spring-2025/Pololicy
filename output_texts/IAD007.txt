PURPOSE: 
To define whom may order and perform diagnostic and interventional 
radiographic procedures. 
POLICY  
A. Any physician or licensed practitioner with ordering privileges, 
credentialed by the Medical Staff office, may order Imaging 
procedures.  
B. Procedure Performance 
1. Routine radiographic studies are performed by a registered 
 radiologic technologist and radiology students under the 
 supervision of a technologist. 
2. Fluoroscopic procedures: Physicians and surgeons whose 
 privileges include performance of procedures requiring use 
of  fluoroscopic equipment and who have demonstrated 
competence in  the use of such equipment in the presence of 
a radiologic  technologist to insure proper radiation safety
