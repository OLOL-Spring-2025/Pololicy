Policies and Procedures Manual Manual:  Infection Control 
Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center Chapter: II 
Subject:  Potted Plants and Cut Flowers in Section:  
               Critical Care Units and Neutropenic  
               Isolation Rooms 
Date of Origination:  November 7, 1996 Supersedes:  
Date of Last Revision: March 20, 2019 Serial Code: IC2008 
Date of Last Review: March 31, 2024 
_______________________________________________________________________ 
 
DISTRIBUTION: 
Nursing Services 
 
FORMULATED BY: 
Infection Prevention and Control 
 
PURPOSE: 
To minimize a source (pathogen reservoir) of microbial contamination that could be disseminated via 
direct or indirect contact, and aerosols. 
 
APPLIES TO: 
Nursing Personnel 
 
RESPONSIBILITY: 
Nursing Personnel 
 
POLICY: 
No potted plants, dried or fresh cut flowers or other form of vegetative plants shall be placed in Critical 
Care patient rooms or in rooms of patients on neutropenic precautions.
