PURPOSE: 
To provide a place for quiet prayer or reflection that acknowledges the cultural, spiritual, and religious 
diversity of our patients, families, visitors, physicians, volunteers, and staff. 
 
POLICY: 
Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center (OLOL) provides two Meditation Rooms for the use of 
patients, families, visitors and employees. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
1. The Meditation Rooms are located on the first floor of the main building across from Elevator E, and 
on the second floor of the HVI adjacent to the waiting area. 
 
2. The Meditation Room on the first floor is available 24 hours a day. The Meditation Room on the 
second floor is available when the waiting area is open. 
 
3. In respect of all, only religious items approved by the Pastoral Care Department may be left in the 
Meditation Rooms.
