Applies to: 
  
Plant Operations 
  
Policy: 
  
To maintain drawings and documents which indicate the location of the controls for a partial or complete 
shutdown of each utility system and to ensure, through training of the Plant Operations Department 
personnel that proper procedures are followed in the shutdown of any utility system component.  
  
Responsibility: 
  
Plant Operations 
  
Procedure: 
  
1.                   Procedures specific to the following utility systems are located in Utilities Management, Utility 
systems included but not limited to: 
  
Steam 
Hot Water 
Water Distribution/Plumbing System 
HVAC System 
Communication System 
Electrical Distribution System 
Elevators 
Fire Alarm System 
Medical Gas Delivery System 
Medical Air System 
Medical Vacuum System 
  
             
  
2.                   When a utility system must be shutdown, it is required that Administration, Department 
Directors, and the Administrative Supervisor be notified.
