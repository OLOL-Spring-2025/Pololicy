PURPOSE: 
To provide method for verifying patient’s release to legal guardian in the inpatient areas only. 
 
POLICY:  
Pediatric patient’s legal guardianship is recorded at the time of admission as stated by accompanying caretaker. 
 
Enter name of legal guardian on pediatric admission face sheet.  Before discharging patient  
verify that child is being released to legal guardian or representative thereof. 
 
If any conflict or concerns arise regarding guardianship, the staff nurse should notify the Charge Nurse who will 
contact the House Supervisor. Charge Nurse will inform the Unit Director.  These individuals may request legal 
verification of guardianship.  
 
Risk Management and the hospital attorney are available resources.
