PURPOSE: 
The purpose of prepping loose batches is to group like document types and expedite the quality review 
of these loose documents. 
 
POLICY: 
Every loose document will be prepped prior to being scanned into EPIC. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
1. Retrieve loose documents from the designated area in the EDM department. 
2. Remove all staples and paper clips. 
3. Remove all blank pages and blank forms. 
4. Remove all carbon copies. 
5. Remove any Clinical Pathway documents and place them in the designated interoffice mail bin. 
6. Repair damaged documents with tape. 
7. Lab and Radiology reports from OLOL  (excluding Transfer information, H&P’s, consents, lab 
values and advance directives) can be placed in the recycle bin. 
8. Arrange like forms within the batch whenever possible.   
9. Limit the number of forms in a loose batch to 50 pages, except for EKG’s, these are limited to 30 
pages. 
10. Report any problems to your supervisor or member of the management team.
