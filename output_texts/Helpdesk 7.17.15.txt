PURPOSE: 
To outline the procedures for trouble shooting system issues and contacting the Helpdesk.  
 
POLICY: 
Access Services Employees are to follow the same general troubleshooting procedures for all 
system related problems to determine if the helpdesk should be contacted.   
 
PROCEDURES: 
 
Contact the Helpdesk immediately if:   
 
Laptop is not holding a charge – Make sure the battery light is on first. 
Nothing is printing or is printing to the wrong location or tray – Be ready to tell them if it is one 
specific system or multiple systems. 
EPIC goes down – Know when, and specify if it is one system or multiple systems 
 
*Please contact the Supervisor on duty/on-call if the situation is not resolved in a timely manner 
or if units become frustrated.  The supervisor will notify the Manager on call if necessary.
