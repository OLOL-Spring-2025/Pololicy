PURPOSE: 
To establish guidelines for suctioning patients without an artificial 
airway. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
A. Respiratory therapists are required to have a physician’s 
order for nasotracheal suctioning.   
B. Therapists may suction the oropharynx without a 
physician’s order as indicated by patient condition. 
C. The therapist must chart in a description of the procedure, 
outcome and any adverse reactions in the patient’s EMR 
D. Suctioning is a shared responsibility between nursing and 
respiratory therapy.
