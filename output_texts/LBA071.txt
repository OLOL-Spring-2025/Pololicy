LABORATORY ADMINISTRATION MANUAL 
LBA071 
 
FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT 
 
 
PURPOSE 
To ensure sound financial management for laboratory operations including Inpatient, 
Outpatient, and Outreach Clients.  To assure compliance with regulatory standards 
related financial operations. 
 
 
POLICY 
Laboratory administration, supervisors, IS managers, accountants, registration clerks, and 
billing clerks are responsible for the on-going review of financial documentation.   
 
Financial Management includes, but is not limited to: 
• CDM review to verify that the current/appropriate CPT Codes are being 
utilized. 
• Education of Clients to ensure proper documentation/utilization of correct 
ICD-10 codes and medical necessity. 
• Pricing of procedure to include cost of reagents, labor, reference lab 
charges, etc. 
• To implement price increases/decreases as appropriate. 
• Audits to assure that all procedures bill correctly (order to bill-drop) 
• Utilization of ABN's in accordance with regulatory standards. 
• Correction of error reports. 
• Compliance with regulatory standards and federally funded programs. 
 
The Laboratory Revenue Management Committee will work with Financial Operations, 
Internal Audit, Medicare Focus Review and others to assure that all financial transactions 
are properly captured, billed, and documented to comply with facility and regulatory 
standards.
