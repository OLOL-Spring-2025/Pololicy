PURPOSE: 
To describe how to use the computer to assign patient draws for AM Rounds utilizing EPIC Hyperspace: Patient 
Draw List 
 
POLICY: 
The night shift charge reviews and assigns the AM rounds for collection by the Phlebotomy Team.  The orders are 
listed by priority, collection time, room number, name, and medical record number (MRN). 
 
A. EPIC Order Release date/time 
a. Released electronically  
b. Done daily at 00:31 and 0505 
B. Orders release for AM rounds at 00:31 and 0505 on the Patient Draw List. 
C. Morning round are assigned utilizing EPIC Hyperspace via the “Patient Draw List”. 
 
Timed Requests during the Morning Round 
A. Timed requests ordered during the hours of 04:00 to 06:30 are included in Morning Rounds and drawn at the 
time ordered 
B. Any PTT, Cardiac Enzyme, Drug Level, or 6th floor timed Calcium order is to be drawn by the morning team 
at the time ordered 
 
Patient Draw List 
A. In the Patient Draw list, click on the column header for the patient room number to sort orders 
numerically by room number 
B. The night shift Charge Tech assigns collections to night shift and morning shift phlebotomist scheduled 
for the day. 
C. The night shift Charge Tech is to print a Patient Draw List after all phlebotomist assignments have been 
made  
D. Place the printed list with the Morning Round Tally Report 
 
Morning Rounds 
A. Night shift personnel are dispatched to draw the Intensive Care units 
a. CICU/SICU are drawn first 
b. MICU/MSCC/TNCC/RCU/PCU are drawn next 
c. Send down blood to the laboratory as each unit is completed
