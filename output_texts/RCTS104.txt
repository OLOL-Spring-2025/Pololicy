POLICY: Rehab Center patients will be assisted in getting transportation to and from physician’s offices 
outside the hospital when deemed appropriate. 
PROCEDURE: 
 
1. The physician will write an order for the scheduled outpatient appointment, stating the means of 
transportation, which is medically needed, as well as stating if the patient needs qualified medical 
help or an attendant. 
2. Rehab Nursing will communicate to the assigned Case Manager when a pass is being granted for a 
patient to attend an outside Physician appointment.  
3. The Case Manager will: 
a. Contact the patient’s family for notification and make arrangements for training, if needed, 
so the family is educated in handling the patient during transport / transfers.  The family is 
the remain with the patient for the entire appointment. 
b. Arrange with Rehab Nursing for the patient to receive a pass for the appointment if 
physician’s office is off-site.  If office is in the Medical Plaza, patient/family must sign out at 
the nurse’s station.
