PURPOSE: 
Handling of Automatic Stop Orders and Medications due for renewal 
 
POLICY: 
A. Pharmacy and/or respiratory therapy shall not discontinue an order without approval from an 
authorized prescriber or as indicated by a medical staff-approved protocol.   
B. Those orders with automatic stop dates will be discontinued by the system at the appointed stop 
date/time without notification to the physician.
