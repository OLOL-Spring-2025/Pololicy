SCOPE: 
Applies to team members who work in or for Our Lady of the Lake Hospital, Inc. (OLOLH) retail/community 
pharmacies. 
PURPOSE: 
To ensure accountability for transfer of filled medications from the possession of the pharmacy to that of the patient. 
POLICY: 
The pharmacy will obtain documentation of receipt of medication for all prescriptions dispensed. 
 
DEFINITIONS: 
N/A 
PROCEDURE: 
[URAC PHARM OP 5 (a-i)] [URAC PHARM OP 15 (a-i)] 
A. Filled medications will be stored in the pharmacy until patient or designee purchases/picks up the medications. 
B. Patient or designee must present government issued identification for all controlled substances and drugs of 
concern.  
C. All patients must sign electronic signature pad or alternate document if applicable acknowledging the receipt of 
their prescriptions.  
D. Electronic signatures or alternate document will remain on file for a period not less than 3 years. 
E. Electronic signature log will be readily retrievable as required by governing agencies and 3rd party auditors. 
F. Medications not picked up 10 days from time of fill will be pulled from prescription rack, the claim will be 
reversed, and the medication returned to stock in accordance with LAPB rules and regulations. 
Note:  The information described herein does not create any legal rights and FMOLHS reserves the right to deviate 
from, revise or eliminate this policy, at any time, in its sole discretion.
