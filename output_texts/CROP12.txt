Policy: 
 
Upon entrance into any maintenance program (Cardiac Maintenance Phase III or Pulmonary 
Rehabilitation Maintenance Level), participants must be designated to have Self -Pay status, unless 
otherwise approved by the business office. 
 
Maintenance program participants whose rehabilitation has been paid for or partially paid for by 
Medicare must sign a Medicare Release form, acknowledging that they are responsible for the 
payment of the bill for their maintenance program. 
 
 
Procedure: 
After appropriate explanation of status change, a patient intending to enter a maintenance program 
with Cardiopulmonary Rehabilitation will sign a Medicare Release form before the initial session of 
then maintenance program. A copy of the Medicare Release form will be kept on the patient's chart. 
 
Admissions is notified of the patient's change in status to Self-Pay, and that the patient will now be a 
recurring maintenance outpatient.
