POLICY: 
The Rehab Center will adhere to current OLOLRMC policies that address patient rights.  In addition, a 
clinician will inform each patient of the following additional rights with respect to Medicare’s IRF-PAI 
prior to performing the assessment. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
 
1. A clinician will inform each patient of the following rights: 
 
• The right to be informed of the purpose of the patient assessment data collection. 
• The right to have any patient assessment information that is collected to remain confidential and 
secure. 
• The right to be informed that the patient assessment information will not be disclosed to others 
except for legitimate purposes allowed by the Federal Privacy Act and Federal and State 
Regulations. 
• The right to refuse to answer patient assessment data questions. 
• The right to see, review, and request changes on the patient assessment instrument. 
 
2. A clinician will document in the Medicare patient’s clinical record that the patient has been informed 
of the above patient rights.
