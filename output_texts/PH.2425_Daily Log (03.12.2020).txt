SCOPE: 
Applies to team members who work in or for Our Lady of the Lake Hospital, Inc. (OLOLH) retail/community 
pharmacies. 
PURPOSE: 
This policy assures that state regulations are being followed in regard to keeping a daily prescription log. 
POLICY: 
Daily prescription logs must be maintained according to State Board of Pharmacy requirements. 
DEFINITIONS: 
N/A 
PROCEDURE: 
A. At the end of every workday, all pharmacists responsible for dispensing prescriptions will sign a log 
attesting that the information entered into the data processing system was reviewed and is correct. 
 
B. The log shall contain the following information: 
 
1. Date 
2. Pharmacist id 
3. Pharmacist signature 
 
C. The log shall be filed and maintained on site in accordance with Louisiana Board of Pharmacy 
regulations. 
Note:  The information described herein does not create any legal rights and FMOLHS reserves the right to deviate 
from, revise or eliminate this policy, at any time, in its sole discretion.
