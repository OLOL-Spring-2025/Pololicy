SCOPE: 
Applies to Franciscan Missionaries of Our Lady Health System, Inc. (FMOLHS) and its wholly owned sponsored 
organizations that propose and adopt System wide policies. 
PURPOSE: 
To provide guidelines for abstracting within the Our Lady of the Lake Cancer Institute (OLOLCI) cancer registry to 
ensure data quality as it relates to coding cancer diagnosis, stage, treatment, and outcomes. 
POLICY: 
 This policy explains the requirement of the Certified Tumor Registrar (CTR) credential for staff abstracting in the 
cancer registry. 
DEFINITIONS: 
  
PROCEDURE: 
I. All cancer registry staff who abstract cases at OLOLCI will either: 
a. Hold a current CTR, or 
b. Perform case abstracting under the supervision of a CTR 
II. Any non-credentialed staff performing abstracting will be supervised by a CTR under the following guidelines:  
a. A minimum of 25% of analytic cases will be reviewed by a CTR. 
b. Review findings will be documented and discussed at least weekly with non-credentialed staff. 
c. Depending on the accuracy rate of findings, additional cases will be reviewed as necessary.  
III. Non-CTRs must pass the CTR examination within three years of the date hired to perform abstracting in order to 
perform analytic case abstracting within the OLOLCI. 
IV. Non-CTRs may perform case finding, follow-up, abstracting of non-analytic cases, and RCRS case monitoring.  
V. Non-CTRs must demonstrate completion of three hours of cancer-related continuing education applicable to their 
roles each calendar year and can include the following topics: 
a. Advances in cancer diagnosis and treatment 
b. Changes in cancer program standards
