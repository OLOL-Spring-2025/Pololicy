Distribution:   Cardiovascular Lab 
 
Formulated By:  Cardiovascular Lab 
 
Purpose:              To eliminate equipment / supply loss and to 
                       facilitate infection control practices. 
 
Applies to:           Patient Care Services, Security, Housekeeping Services 
 
Policy:              The Cardiovascular Laboratory access 
 
Procedure: 
 
1. There should be limited access to the Cardiovascular Lab after hours to facilitate infection 
control practices.  If any work is done in the Cardiovascular Labs after hours, a terminal 
room clean is necessary prior to performing the next case. 
2. If a supply / piece of equipment is borrowed after hours, an “Internal departmental supply / 
non tissue implant borrow form” (Borrow form) needs to be completed and left at the Cath 
Lab desk.  The “Borrow forms” are located at the Cath Lab desk.
