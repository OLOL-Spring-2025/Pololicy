POLICY   
 
PGL will not perform autopsies on deceased patients under investigation (PUI) or persons positive for 
Crutzfeldt-Jacob disease due to the limitations of environmental controls at Our Lady of the Lake, Baton 
Rouge General MidCity and Woman’s Hospital autopsy suites and/or grossing area. 
 
If there is any suspicion of Crutzfeldt-Jacob disease, the autopsy would be shipped out to a contracted 
provider.   
 
• Refer to the attached procedure from our current local autopsy provider.   
 
• Refer to Case Western Reserve University- National Prion Disease Pathology Surveillance Center 
 
o https://case.edu/medicine/pathology/divisions/prion-center
