POLICY: 
In order to reduce medication errors and support patient safety, the hospital standardizes and/or limits the 
number of drug concentrations available.  Guidelines for PICU and Neonatal Continuous Infusions are 
recommended for safe medication administration. 
 
Standard dilutions and concentrations for pediatric and neonatal emergency drip preparations are attached.  If 
other concentrations are prescribed, the physician should be contacted by a pharmacist or nurse to change orders 
to the standards in this policy.
