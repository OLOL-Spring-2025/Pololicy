PURPOSE: 
To state the procedure for opening and closing nursing units. 
 
POLICY: 
Patient Care Services will open and/or close a nursing unit at the direction of Nursing Administration in 
consultation with Hospital Administration in accordance with the following procedure. 
 
PROCEDURES: 
A. The decision is communicated to Director of Nursing/House Manager Designee or Charge Nurse 
(CN) of the time and date of the unit opening or closing. 
 
B. The Director of Nursing/House Manager /Designee will inform the following departments: 
a. Access Services 
b. Patient Care Services 
c. Nursing Informatics 
d. Biomedical Services 
e. Care Management 
f. IS Support Communications 
g. Financial Operations 
h. Food Service 
i. Laboratory 
j. Radiology 
k. Respiratory Care 
l. Guest Services 
m. Health Information Management 
n. Housekeeping 
o. Linen Services 
p. Marketing 
q. Materials Management 
r. Pastoral Care 
s. Pharmacy 
t. Plant Services 
u. Regulatory 
v. Revenue Management 
w. Security  
x. Infection Control  NNA-077

y. Licensing 
 
 
C. When the decision to close a unit for greater than four (4) days, the closing unit will:  
  
a. Inform pharmacy, will return all discharged patient medications to pharmacy or transfer 
the medication with the patient to another unit. 
b. The nursing unit will close all doors to patient rooms, store equipment, clear hallways. 
c. The medication cart will be locked and clear of all medications. 
d. All computers will be locked out and medical records sent to HIM.
