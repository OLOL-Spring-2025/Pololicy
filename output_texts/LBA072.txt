BUDGET 
 
 
PURPOSE 
To provide a financial foundation for Laboratory operations and procurement of capital 
equipment.  Operations budget represents funding related to laboratory operation.  The 
capital equipment budget represents funding available for the purchase/replacement of 
equipment valued at greater than $1500.00. 
 
 
POLICY 
Laboratory administration, supervisors, and managers prepare drafts of operational and 
capital budgets for submission to Senior Management.  Drafts are reviewed, revised, and 
approved. 
 
Laboratory administration, supervisors, and managers are accountable for departmental 
financial management   Reports utilized by management for sound financial management 
include, but not limited to, include: 
 
• Daily Revenue and Usage Reports 
• ATM payroll data 
• Procedure/billable flex staffing 
• Productivity reports 
• HBSI report 
• Budget standards
