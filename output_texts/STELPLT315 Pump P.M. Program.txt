POLICY: 
  
There is a visual inspection completed daily and adjustments or corrective action is taken 
as needed. There are also monthly servicing to the units. 
  
SCOPE: 
  
Applies to all Plant Operations team members so to maintain equipment efficiency and to 
prevent sudden breakdowns. 
  
PROCEDURE: 
  
            Daily Preventative Maintenance 
  
            1.         Visually inspect pump and motor unit for leaks, vibrations or any other problems. 
            2.         Take corrective action as needed. 
            3.         Record any adjustments made. 
  
            Monthly Preventative Maintenance 
  
            1.         Record suction and discharge pressure if possible. 
2.           Record ampere. 
3.        Add Lubrication to pump and motor as needed. 
                         NOTE:  Do not over lubricate. 
            4.         Keep units clean.
