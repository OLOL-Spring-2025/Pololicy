PURPOSE:  
To ensure proper procedure on admitting patients when a Physician has been placed in a 
suspended status 
 
PROCEDURE:  
If Admissions Rep becomes aware that a physician has been suspended, the Admissions Rep will 
not list that physician in the registration system while registering a patient. The Admissions Rep 
will contact Medical Staff office to verify how they are to proceed.
