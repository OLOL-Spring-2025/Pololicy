Purpose:              To define hours of operation of the Cardiovascular Laboratory. 
 
Applies to:           Cardiovascular Lab 
 
Policy:                
 
1. Hours of operation for the Cardiovascular Lab are as follows: 
a. Normal working hours are 6:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Monday through Friday. CVL will 
observe OLOL holidays with on-call coverage. 
b. On-call coverage for emergencies and/or scheduled cases is provided after working 
hours, and on weekends and holidays. 
c. During periods of decreased volume of procedures, the Cath Lab may close early at the 
discretion of the Director/Supervisor. On-call coverage will be available during these 
periods. 
 
Our Lady of the Lake Ascension: OLOLA 
1. Normal hours of operation will be 6:00 am to 2:30 pm Monday through Friday. 
2. There is no on call coverage. 
3. Cases will be scheduled during these times with the last case being posted, no later than 
1:30 pm start time. 
4. If the volume of the procedures is decreased, the lab may close early at the discretion of 
the Supervisor. 
5. Pre/Post area will be staffed from 6:00 am Monday through Friday until the estimated 
discharge time of the last patient of the day.
