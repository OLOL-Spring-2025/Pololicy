 Procedure:  
 
           EXTENSION CORDS 
 
I. Extension Cords are not permitted in patient areas. Patient areas shall include 
waiting rooms, patient rooms and critical care areas. 
 
Exception: Temporary emergency use is permitted.  Temporary cords 
shall use only three wire grounded construction (16 gauge minimum) . 
A person should be able to remove them without the use of a tool. You 
can’t affix them to a desk, wall, cart, podium, etc. with screws, zip ties 
or glue. Velcro is acceptable.  
 
           II. Extension Cords are permitted in office areas where patients do not normally 
enter. Power strips/surge protection used permanently in these areas shall be of 
the multiple outlet type which is protected by an overload device and having a 
UL rating.  
 
III. Extensions cords must be inspected and approved by Plant Operations before use.
