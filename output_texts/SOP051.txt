PURPOSE: 
To state the processes and supporting functions for the delivery of Autotransfusion blood in the 
Operating Room. 
 
POLICY: 
1. All autotransfusion blood administration will be determined by the operating surgeon and 
managed by the attending anesthesia personnel. 
2. Operation of the autotransfusion equipment in the perioperative setting is performed by 
OLOL Perfusion personnel as well as the perioperative Anesthesia Techs.  
3. Initial operator training utilizes a proctored method by experienced autotransfusion 
personnel. Initial operator competency is assessed and documented using the criteria found 
in the Haemonetics Cell Saver 5 Autologous Blood Recovery System Operator’s Manual. 
4. Microaggregate filtration of 20 to 40 microns should be performed on all reinfused blood. 
5. Reinfusion of blood from this collection system must be within 4 hours from the completion 
of collection. (AABB Guidelines 3rd Edition 2007) 
6. Post transfusion disposal of autotransfusion tubing components and any residual blood 
products or wash residue shall be accomplished by depositing all items in a large 
Biohazardous container with sufficient solidifying agent. The container is then discarded 
following standard Biohazardous disposal methods. 
7. Ongoing and issue related servicing of the Autologous Blood Recovery System units is the 
responsibility of the OLOL Biomedical Department with the technical support of the 
manufacturer if needed. 
 
 
REFERENCES: 
      1.  Cell Saver 5 Autologous Blood Recovery System Operator’s Manual 
      2.  AABB Guidelines 3rd Edition 2007
