POLICY: 
  
 There are monthly tests and inspections performed on the MED GAS ALARM  systems 
throughout the hospital.  Corrective action will be taken as needed. 
  
PROCEDURE: 
  
            Monthly Preventative Maintenance: 
 
 AMICO PANELS: 
             
1. Visually check if green normal light is on and pressure gauges are showing  
             within range. 
2.               Push test button.  All lights should be lit & audio alarm should sound. 
3.               Take corrective action as needed, call for service if any lights or alarm fails. 
 Evergreen Medical 1-800-770-1503 
 Preventative Maintenance for Gulf Coast 235-806-0785 
 
BEACON MEDAES PANELS: 
 
1.       Visually check if green normal light is on and pressure gauges are showing   
       within range. 
2.        Press test and hold for 5 seconds. System will go through a self check, watch   
       to make sure all modules function correctly. Audio should sound. 
3.        Take corrective action as needed, call for service if any lights or alarms fail. 
              Evergreen Medical 1-800-770-1503 
  Preventative Maintenance for Gulf Coast 235-806-0785
