PURPOSE: 
To establish a work flow process to verify that special supplies, implants and/or equipment are 
available as requested for a case. 
 
POLICY: 
All specialty supplies, implants and/or equipment request for certain procedures should be handled 
according to the following procedure. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
1. The physicianâ€™s office posting the case should identify any special items that are needed for 
a surgical procedure at time of posting. 
2. The posting staff will verify these items as identified in the comments section when the 
procedure is posted. 
3. The Nurse Manager/Nurse Supervisor/Charge Nurse of each OR specialty service will 
verify requested items by checking daily or within 72 hours of all scheduled procedures. 
4. If during the verification process, it is identified that an item is not available, the physician 
will be notified immediately so other preparations can be made with another vendor or said 
procedure re-scheduled. 
5. All new product supply requests should be handled through the new product process.
