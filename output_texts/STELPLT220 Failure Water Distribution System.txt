Policy: 
  
In the event of a malfunction and/or failure of the water distribution system, the 
following procedure will be followed: 
  
Responsibility: 
  
Plant Operations 
  
Procedure: 
  
1.                  When disruption to or breakage of the main water line into hospital occurs: 
  
A. Notify the Administrative  House Supervisor/designee that the reserve 
water supply is being used and that water rationing 
must be put into effect. 
  
2.                  When breakage of or distribution to inside water pipes occurs: 
  
A.             Locate the point of breakage. 
  
B.             Notify the effected areas of the problem and estimate the down time if 
possible. 
  
C.             The Plant Operations Department personnel will make the necessary 
repairs whenever possible. If the repairs are outside the ability of the Plant 
Operations Department personnel, see “Utility Emergency Call Sheet” 
located in Plant Operations.

FAILURE WATER DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM 
  
  
3.                  Contamination of the water supply. 
  
A.             Turn off the main domestic water entry valve. 
  
B.  Instruct all personnel and visitors through the public address system “DO 
NOT” drink the water or flush the toilets. 
  
C.        Contact the Administrative House Supervisor/designee to notify the   
       Department of Health (225-644-5916) immediately of water supply 
            contamination. 
  
D.              Contact the City of Gonzales @ 647-2841. 
  
E. Will contact FMOLHS Materials Management Department to order the    
             necessary bottled water for our facility or will request delivery of water   
             from outside vendor in accordance with agreement through Kentwood    
            Water Company @ 647-1666 or 225-751-8495
