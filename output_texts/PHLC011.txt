PURPOSE: 
To allow timely removal of the Blood Bank Identification Armband  
 
Addresses: 
Supervisors 
Venipuncture Charge Techs 
Venipuncture Phlebotomists 
 
POLICY: 
Patient cross matches may be set up on a draw in lab sample for three days.  After three days, a patient may begin 
to produce antibodies to any unit received and consequently must be redrawn and cross matched using a newly 
collected sample. 
A. Patients with the following orders are to have Blood Bank armband and packet 
• Crossmatch with units 
• Type and antibody screen 
• Draw and Hold order 
B. The following information is required to be present on the Blood Bank armband 
• Blood Bank Identification System  packet number 
• Date 
• Time of draw 
• Network ID of person drawing the sample of crossmatch 
C. Blood Bank armbands may be removed by the following personnel 
• Venipuncture Techs 
• Blood Bank personnel 
• Nursing personnel upon the patient’s discharge from the hospital 
D. The armband is valid for three days. 
E. The Phlebotomist is to check for the presence of a Blood Bank  armband before drawing an order requiring 
the placement of a Blood Bank armband 
F. The patient being drawn has an armband and the band is dated: 
• Less than or three days old 
o Do not draw the patient 
o Return the order to Collections as blood in lab 
• More than three days old – draw the patient, remove the armband present  
o Cut off the existing armband 
o Draw the patient 
o Establish a new Blood Bank Identification armband and packet OUR LADY OF THE LAKE RMC
