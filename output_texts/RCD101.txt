PURPOSE: To ensure appropriate utilization of treatment upon receipt of orders. 
POLICY: Physical Therapy, Speech Therapy, Occupational Therapy services will be administered only 
following the receipt of a physician’s order. 
PROCEDURE: 
 
 
1. A referral is received from physicians to initiate patient evaluations and/or treatment.  The order may be 
written (medical chart), verbal, or by telephone read- back.  Verbal orders must be documented in the chart as 
follows: “Verbal (or phone) order/referring physician/therapist signature/ RB”. 
2. All orders shall be confirmed by a licensed therapist prior to evaluation and treatment. 
3. Those orders that specify “evaluation only” or “recommendation only” shall not proceed to patient treatment 
unless additional orders stating such are received from a physician.  Evaluations/Recommendations reports 
may be placed on patient’s chart to be reviewed by physicians or information may be called to physician at his 
office.   
4. Request for changes or additions to patient’s order(s) must be approved by the referring physician. 
5. Any discipline may make recommendations for referral to a physician based on criteria outlined in division-
specific scopes of service (e.g. – difficulty swallowing may warrant a referral by the PT for ST services, poor 
appetite may warrant a referral by therapy to nutritional services, etc.).
