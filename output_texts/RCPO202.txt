POLICY: 
 It is the policy of the Rehabilitation Center to ensure that visiting hours enhances the recovery of each patient and 
does not detract from therapeutic efforts and goals of the treatment plan.  Rehabilitation Center staff will enforce 
visiting hours in a consistent manner.  Exceptions can and will be made either to accommodate the extenuating 
circumstances of a particular family or to promote therapeutic efforts 
PROCEDURE: 
 
The visiting hours of the Rehabilitation Center are from 3:30 p.m. to 9:00 p.m. throughout the week with the 
understanding that therapy schedules may vary from weekdays to weekends.  Rest breaks are provided between 
12:00pm to 1:30pm.  Families will be made aware of this policy upon initial contact with nursing and Case 
Management.  Families and friends will be asked to visit patients in their room or other designated visiting areas.  
Visitors will be discouraged from entering the gymnasium without prior arrangement with their patient, case 
manager or rehabilitation physician.  At times families and significant other s may be invited to observe the 
patient in therapy, and this will be arranged by the appropriate treatment team member.  Visitors who enter the 
gymnasium without prior clearance will be politely asked by staff members to leave in order to protect 
confidentiality and the rights of all patients treated in the gym.
