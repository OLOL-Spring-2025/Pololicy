PURPOSE: 
 
To assure the Medical Staff electronic voting process is standard, fair and appropriate. 
 
POLICY: 
 
Electronic voting via email or electronic survey can be used for some decision making processes in 
which the decision is important to have all members participate.   
 
 
PROCEDURE: 
 
1. Each service can determine whether or not they choose to use an electronic voting process. 
 
2. A request for an electronic vote must be made by a member of the service and approved by the 
chief of service if it meets the standards below. 
 
3. If such request is made, the Medical Staff Office will e-mail the proposal to all service members 
for their review and allow a specified number of days for the service member s to review and 
return the proposal electronically. 
 
4. E-mail is the only way that the Medical Staff Office has of verifying authenticity; therefore, a 
fax or proxy will not be accepted. 
 
5. Electronic voting is not allowed for any elections.  This is in accordance with the Medical Staff 
Bylaws.
