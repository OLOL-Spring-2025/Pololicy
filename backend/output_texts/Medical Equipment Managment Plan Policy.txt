1.PURPOSE -- SCOPE
To ensure that each facility managed by TRIMEDX has an updated Medical 
Equipment Management Plan and an Annual Assessment submitted annually 
to the facility's EOC/Safety Committee.

3.DEFINITIONS
3.1. Medical Equipment Management Plan – A document that outlines the 
policies and procedures that meet the site specific regulatory and 
accreditation requirements
3.2. Annual Assessment – A quantitative indicator report that reflects 
regulatory and accreditation metrics, which serves as a single source for 
program effectiveness

5.RESPONSIBILITIES
5.1.TRIMEDX Quality and Regulatory Compliance Department
5.1.1. Review all applicable federal and state regulations and 
accreditation agency standards for any changes that relate to or 
could affect the MEMP content and/or implementation
5.1.2. Update MEMP and Annual Assessment templates on an annual 
basis; post MEMP templates in the TRIMEDX Quality Management 
System (ETQ) 
5.1.3. Provide guidance to Site Leadership during MEMP and Annual 
Assessment review
5.1.4. Determine required due date each year for MEMP and Annual 
Assessments
5.1.5. Collect signed MEMP and an Annual Assessment from all Site 
Managers annually, by the required due date
5.2.TRIMEDX Site Managers 
5.2.1. Review and update the facility's MEMP and Annual Assessment 
with Annual Indicators annually
5.2.2. Insert facility Letterhead at the top of the MEMP template
5.2.3. Present MEMP and Annual Assessment to facility representative 
annually
5.2.4. Ensure that a facility representative signs the MEMP or provide 
EOC meeting minutes approving the MEMP.
5.2.5. Submit signed MEMP and a completed Annual Assessment to 
TRIMEDX Quality and Regulatory Compliance Department annually
5.2.6. Submit signed MEMP and a completed Annual Assessment with 
Annual Indicators to Manager, Quality and Regulatory Compliance 
Department annually by the due date. Sites must submit an approved 
MEMP and Annual Assessment by due date determined by the 
Quality and Regulatory Compliance Department

6.COMPLETE POLICY
6.1. It is policy, per state regulations and/or accreditation agency standards, 
that all facilities utilizing patient care equipment have a Medical 
Equipment Management Plan (MEMP) in writing and that the Plan be 
reviewed and updated annually (Annual Assessment).
6.2. The MEMP and the Annual Assessment are owned and accepted by the 
facility in accordance with contract agreements and regulatory 
requirements. The submission of the approved MEMP and Annual 
Assessment will occur at minimum by the due date specified by the 
Quality and Regulatory Compliance Department.
