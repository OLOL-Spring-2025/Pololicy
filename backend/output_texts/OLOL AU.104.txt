SET UP FOR AUTOPSY 
 
 
1. Access Autopsy suite 
• Badge access to enter 
• Keys for drawers and cabinets in histology office 
 
2. Set up the following instruments in the tray: 
• scalpel blades and handles 
• 1 hammer 
• 1 rib cutter 
• 1 rib knife 
• 1 double edge flat knife 
• long dissecting blades 
• 1 smooth forceps 
• 1 chisel 
• 3 scissors 
• 1 long knife 
• 1 cup 
 
3. Gather specimen containers and label appropriately with two patient identifiers and site 
 
4. Document autopsy number on autopsy consent 
 
5. Document patient name, autopsy number and pathologist initials in the autopsy log book 
 
6. Put the “Room in Use” sign outside the autopsy suite entrance 
 
7. Deliver any paperwork or information to appropriate pathologist
