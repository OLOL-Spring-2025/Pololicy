Title: Emergency Access to Endoscopy Policy Reference #: SYA005 
Supersedes #:  
Date of Origination: 07/25/1988 Review Date: 2/14/2024 
Revision Date: 03/15/19 
 
 
PURPOSE: 
To define level of service available to Endoscopy 
 
POLICY:   
Endoscopy will be staffed and equipped to allow prompt access to surgical and anesthesia 
service for Emergency patients. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
 
1. Endoscopy is staffed 24 hours a day by scheduled or on-call RN, Backup RN and 
Endoscopy Technician. 
2. The Physician will notify the on-call RN, or House Supervisor, only if the RN is needed 
to respond immediately to the emergent situation.   
3. In the event an emergency case must interrupt the routine schedule, the procedure for 
bumping a case is followed.
