PURPOSE: 
To assure an appropriate, proper employee response in the performance of assigned work duties 
 
Disciplinary action in the form of a “Counseling Action” may result for employee failure to follow policy 
guidelines of Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center and the Venipuncture Department.  Disciplinary 
actions may result from but are not limited to the following: 
 
A. Gross Misconduct : 
Theft, obscenity, verbal or physical abuse, discrimination, sexual harassment, possession of weapons, 
destruction of property, substance abuse 
 
B. Unprofessional Behavior : 
 Discourteous behavior towards co-workers or patients, excessive personal phone calls, insubordination, 
sleeping on the job, inappropriate attire, inappropriate appearance 
 
C. Absenteeism : 
No call no show, job abandonment, failure to report to work, leaving work without permission, excessive 
absence, inappropriate absence, tardiness, failure to follow departmental call-in procedures, failure to 
respond to call, unexcused absences 
 
D. Performance: 
Failure to follow work orders, inefficient use of work time, failure to properly complete assigned tasks or 
duties, failure to follow Venipuncture departmental policy 
 
E. Documentation: 
Failure to follow departmental documentation standards 
 
F. Time Reporting: 
Falsification of time reporting, failure to follow ATM/KRONOS procedure, repeated failure to clock in 
using ATM/KRONOS procedure 
 
G. Licensure: 
Failure to renew or maintain state laboratory assistant license 
 
H. Other Violations: 
Behaviors not specifically noted but result in failure to follow policy and procedure
