PURPOSE: 
             To establish guidelines concerning Imaging On-Call and Call-back coverage.  
 
POLICY: 
A. Should a radiographic service need to be placed on a “call-back” basis (CT, NM, US, 
Specials,MRI), a technologist will be assigned to be On-Call. 
  
B. It is policy that a response time of  30 MINUTES be adhered to by the technologist on-call. 
  
C. The technologist on-call must maintain updated phone information, and present information 
regarding a change in phone locations to the Imaging Manager or Director and update the on call 
list when appropriate. 
  
D. Any change in technologist coverage (sick, swapping with other techs) must first be approved and 
proper notification of schedule change is reported.
