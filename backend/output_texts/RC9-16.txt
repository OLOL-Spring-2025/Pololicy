PURPOSE: 
To identify which blood gas machine analyzed a specific patient’s blood gas results 
 
PROCEDURES 
A. Access the Aqure Blood Gas Information System. 
B. Click on Patient Results Tab 
C.        Select the appropriate hospital and device.  
D. Select the appropriate date range for sample analyzed. 
E.        Click on Filter. 
F.        Review the accession number or medical record number if applicable. 
G. In this view, the following is displayed: analysis time, approval status, sample 
type, patient ID, patient first and last name, operator, and device. 
H.  Choose the sample you want to review. Once you select the results, you can view 
the device demographics (blood gas analyzer) in order to correlate the patient’s 
blood gas results to the appropriate blood gas analyzer.
