SCOPE: 
Applies to team members who work in or for Our Lady of the Lake Hospital, Inc. (OLOLH) retail/community 
pharmacies. 
PURPOSE: 
This policy assures that the pharmacy conducts monthly audits of patient records to ensure all required content is present 
in each record.  
 
POLICY: 
The pharmacy will ensure accuracy of patient records through a monthly audit process 
 
DEFINITIONS: 
N/A 
PROCEDURE: 
A. Description of Indicator and Threshold/Goal: 
1. 90% of all patient records will include all required information.  
2. If goals are not achieved there will be a written plan of correction. 
3. Goal will be re-evaluated if acceptable limits are not met on a consistent basis as the goal may 
have been set too high. 
 
B. Frequency of Activities: 
Data will be collected quarterly.   
 
C. Responsibility:  
The Pharmacy Manager has been designated the responsibility for collecting, tracking and 
trending data as well as presenting it to the Quality Management Committee (QMC) for review.  
 
D. Methods of Data Collection: 
1. Each quarter a minimum of ten (10) patient records will be reviewed for accuracy using the 
Chart Audit tool. 
2. Audit results will be documented and retained.
