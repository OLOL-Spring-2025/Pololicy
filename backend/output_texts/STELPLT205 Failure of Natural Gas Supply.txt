Policy: 
  
In the event of a malfunction and/or failure of the natural gas supply, the following 
procedure will be followed. 
  
Responsibility: 
  
Plant Operations 
  
Procedure: 
  
1.                  Notify the Manager of Facilities/and or Supervisor/designee immediately. 
  
2.                  Call the City of Gonzales @ 647-2841 (Gas Company) for immediate service. 
  
3.                  The Manager of Facilities along with the Supervisor will determine the   
              length of down time and will notify: 
  
All Departments Within the Hospital  
 
4.         Notify the above listed departments when service is restored.
