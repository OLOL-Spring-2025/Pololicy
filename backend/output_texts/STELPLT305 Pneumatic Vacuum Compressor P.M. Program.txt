POLICY: 
  
            There are readings taken and operational checks made daily.  Monthly and annual 
servicing is performed on all pneumatic vacuum compressors. 
  
SCOPE: 
  
Applies to all Engineering team members so to maintain equipment efficiency and to prevent sudden 
breakdowns. 
  
PROCEDURE: 
  
            Daily Preventative Maintenance 
  
            1.  Record running vacuum pressures. 
            2.  Check oil level at sight glass. 
            3.  Note run time on each unit 
  
            Monthly Preventative Maintenance 
  
             1.    Check overall operations. 
2.     Lubricate areas as needed. 
 3.    Correct all air or oil leaks. 
4.  Clean equipment.  UNITS MUST BE KEPT CLEAN. 
5.  Oil is changed very six (6) weeks. 
6.  Check gauges, lights and alarms 
 
Annual Preventative Maintenance (to be completed by a qualified Med Vac Vendor) 
  
 1.   Inspect starter contacts for wear.  Clean as needed. 
 2.   Check for loose connections in the plumbing and electrical systems and correct. 
3. Repaint any other accessories if paint is chipping or corrosion is found  
4. Read motor ampere.  All leads should read close to the same.  Take corrective action as needed.      
5. Filters are changed annually for each compressor. 
  
RESPONSIBILITY:   Plant Operations
