PURPOSE: 
To provide information for the cleaning and disinfection of the rechargeable surgical 
clipper system. 
 
POLICY: 
The rechargeable surgical clipper system shall be cleaned and disinfected after each 
patient use. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
1. Remove the disposable clipper blade and discard in appropriate biohazard sharps 
container. 
2. Rinse the surgical clipper handpiece with water. 
3. Dry the surgical clipper handpiece and disinfect with a hospital-approved 
germicidal wipe or solution.  (May be completely submersed in water or 
disinfectant). 
4. Replace surgical clipper handpiece into the recharging base.
