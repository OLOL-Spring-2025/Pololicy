SCOPE: 
Applies to team members who work in or for Our Lady of the Lake Hospital, Inc. (OLOLH) retail/community 
pharmacies. 
PURPOSE: 
Applies to team members who work in or for Rx ONE pharmacies. 
POLICY: 
The pharmacy has procedures in place to ensure the proper destruction of PHI/IIHI. 
 
DEFINITIONS: 
PHI – Protected Health Information 
IIHI – Individually Identifiable Health Information 
PROCEDURE: [URAC CPE 1-1 (a)] [URAC RM 3-1 (b)]          
A. As a limited delegated function, the pharmacy has engaged Tiger Shredding to be the document 
shredding provider.  
1. The vendor storage receptacles are secured onsite.  
a. Storage receptacles are locked. 
(1) Storage receptacles are located in the main pharmacy area  
(2) Pharmacy office area if applicable to site 
2. A representative of Tiger Shredding comes to the sites above to pick up the storage receptacles.  
a. Items from the locked bins are collected by representative from Tiger Shredding  
b. Items are transported through use of opaque bags    
3. Destruction of PHI is performed offsite  
a. Items are incinerated according to policy.   
 
B. Records Management 
1. The pharmacy will use the services of Iron Mountain Document storage for offsite storage of 
records. 
2. Records older than 3 years will be packaged in containers provided by Iron Mountain Document 
Storage. 
3. Records will be logged and retrieved from Iron Mountain as required 
4. Records older than 10 years will be destroyed per Iron Mountain destruction policies and 
procedures.
