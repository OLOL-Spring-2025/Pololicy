Distribution:        Cardiovascular Lab (CVL) 
 
Formulated by:       Cardiovascular Lab 
 
Purpose:              To provide a method of staff assignments 
                       in the Cardiovascular Lab. 
 
Applies to:           Cardiovascular Lab Staff 
 
Policy:               All patient care assignments should be made by the     
                       Director, Supervisor &/or Charge Person. 
 
Procedure:             
 
1. The following will be considered when scheduling: 
a. Individual patient acuity and needs. 
b. Procedure to be performed. 
c. Equipment to be used. 
d. Only RN’s will administer medication 
e. Staff’s knowledge and expertise. 
 
2. Additional staff will be assigned as determined by the Supervisor or charge person. 
 
3. At any time, the Supervisor or Charge Person determines that the available staff is unable to 
properly manage the case load, he/she will notify the Director. The Director will make 
adjustments in the staffing patterns as needed, or in cooperation with the physician to 
reschedule the procedure.
