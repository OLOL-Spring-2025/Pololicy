POLICY: 
  
There are monthly inspections performed on the Auto Closing Smoke Doors throughout 
the hospital; corrective action will be taken as needed. 
  
SCOPE: 
  
Applies to all Plant Operations team members so to maintain the Fire Doors, with the use 
of this program, patients, employees and medical staff will be safely housed at Our Lady 
of the Lake Ascension. 
  
RESPONSIBILITY: 
             
            Plant Operations 
             
PROCEDURE: 
  
            Monitored and recorded during each “fire drill”.  Note: one per shift per quarter. 
  
            1.  Record the auto closing smoke asset door numbers and location. 
            2.  Release the door and make sure it latches on top and bottom. If there are no latches   
                 ensure that the door closes freely and completely. 
            3.  Check and tighten all hardware. 
            4.  Take corrective action as needed.
