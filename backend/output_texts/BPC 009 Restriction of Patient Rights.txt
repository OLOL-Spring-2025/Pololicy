POLICY: 
Patients’ rights are protected and exercised as provided by Louisiana Revised Statutes 28:171 
1. Therapeutic indications may necessitate limitations or restrictions of a patient’s rights. 
2. The patient and his/her family, as appropriate, participate in the determination of the limits or 
restrictions. 
3. A physician’s order is obtained including the rationale for the limitation or restriction. 
4. Limits or restrictions are fully explained to the patient and his/her family as appropriate. 
a. The patient’s legal counsel, should he/she have one, must be notified. 
b. Notification to all parties must be in writing, including the reasons. 
5. The therapeutic effectiveness of the limits or restrictions is reviewed at least every 3-days by the 
clinical staff and the need to continue or cancel the limits/restrictions is documented in the 
patient’s medical record.
