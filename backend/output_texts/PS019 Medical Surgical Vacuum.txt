Title: Medical Surgical Vacuum 
Revision Date: 02/08/2024 
Review Date: 09/30/2021 
Reference # PS019 Origin Date: August 1988 
 
PURPOSE: 
To provide a safe physical environment for patients, staff and visitors at all Our Lady of the Lake 
Regional Medical Center (OLOL) network locations. 
 
POLICY: 
Medical vacuum systems are provided by OLOL to adequately service hospital needs. Systems provide 
dual pumps for alternating cycles and stand-by isolation purposes. Four (4) separate systems are 
available and can be tied together by a by-pass valve. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
1. Vacuum systems are maintained and inspected annually. 
2. Alarm systems are installed in the Plant Services Control Room and PBX to alert staff of a vacuum 
loss. 
3. Cross-valving is provided and labeled to connect alternate vacuum systems. 
4. Upon loss of vacuum, Plant Services will open the by-pass valve and connect the system, then shut 
down the inoperative system.
