PURPOSE: 
To establish staffing standards, which provide safe and effective patient care for patients 
undergoing procedures operative and invasive. 
 
POLICY: 
A. The Operating Room Team will minimally consist of the Circulating role and the Scrub 
Technician. 
 
B. The Circulating role will be filled by a Registered Nurse. Only registered nurses employed 
by OLOL or other services contracted by OLOL may be the primary nurse/circulator.  
These RN's must maintain all competencies as outlined by the Surgical Services division for 
the circulating nurse. 
 
C. The scrub role may be a filled by a RN (with a scrub competency) or surgical tech. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
1. All patient care assignments should be made by the Nurse Leader, Nurse Supervisor, or 
designee with consideration of the following: 
a. Individual patient acuity and needs 
b. Procedure to be performed 
c. Equipment technology to be used 
d. Staff's member competency 
2. Additional staff will be assigned as determined by the Nurse Leader or Designee.
