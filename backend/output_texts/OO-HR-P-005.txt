PURPOSE 
The purpose of this policy is to describe the process that will be followed regarding employee 
garnishments, support orders and levies. 
 
 
POLICY 
Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center (OLOL) honors garnishments, support orders and levies 
that are legally incurred against employees. Payroll will conduct and implement all informational and 
compliance activities as required by law.  The Payroll Department maintains the original papers. 
 
 
PROCEDURES 
 
TAX LEVIES: Tax levies are received only through the Legal Services Department. The documents are 
logged and the original is forwarded to Payroll for processing. Interrogatories are completed and sent to 
the appropriate authority. The employee is notified by Payroll via Team Mail (email) memo explaining 
the paperwork received and the amount of the deduction. Payroll will calculate the deduction and 
process the payments to be sent to the appropriate agency. Deductions remain in effect until a release is 
received. 
 
GARNISHMENTS AND SUPPORT ORDERS: Garnishments and support orders are received only 
through the Legal Services Department. The documents are logged and the original is forwarded to 
Payroll for processing. Interrogatories are completed and sent to the appropriate authority. The 
employee is notified by Payroll via Team Mail (email) memo explaining the paperwork received and the 
amount of the deduction. Payroll will calculate the deduction and process the payments to be sent to the 
appropriate agency. Deductions remain in effect until a release is received.
