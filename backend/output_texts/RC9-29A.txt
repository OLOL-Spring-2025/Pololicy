POLICY: 
 The Respiratory Therapy Blood Gas Supervisor will review all records relating to quality control and 
survey results.  The Laboratory  Director of the Blood Gas Laboratory or his/her designee will 
subsequently review this information. 
 
 The Laboratory Director or his/her designee will perform biennial review of all departmental policies 
and laboratory reporting forms biennial and with any change prior to their implementation. In the 
event of a change in Laboratory Director all policies must be reviewed within 3 months and biennial 
thereafter.  
 
 Personnel (Blood Gas Lab Supervisor) in charge of technical operations for the blood gas section will 
have education and experience equivalent to an MT or certified or registered respiratory therapist and 
at least 4 years of experience.  
 
Explanations, corrective actions or results of investigation will be written on the specific report. 
Corrective actions are also recorded in the epoc RALS system.Review will be on a monthly basis.
