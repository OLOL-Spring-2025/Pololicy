PURPOSE:  
This policy establishes guidelines for volunteers working in the Acute Care Physical Medicine Department. 
 
DEFINITIONS: 
None 
 
POLICY:  
It is the policy of the Acute Care Physical Medicine Department to provide opportunities for volunteers 
in the Acute Care Medicine Department. 
 
PROCEDURES: 
 
1. Volunteers are interviewed and approved by the Central Volunteer office of the hospital and referred to 
Physical Medicine/Rehab as appropriate. 
2. Volunteers are oriented by a designated PMR employee and are allowed to perform the following duties: 
a. Assist therapist with patient ambulation by pushing IV pole, Oxygen tanks, wheelchairs 
b. Assist with clerical duties 
c. Assist with equipment cleaning and inventory 
d. Run in-house errands 
e. Maintain tidiness of treatment areas 
f. Assist with filing  
g. Other duties as trained and assigned by the therapist 
3.  Volunteers are responsible for signing in/out of department on the volunteer sign-in sheet.
