PURPOSE: 
To provide pertinent information regarding team member holidays. 
 
POLICY:  
1. Holidays are defined as a Day or Evening shift on the day of the holiday and a Night shift on the night 
before the holiday. 
2. Full-time staff members are required to work 24 hours of the following major holidays based on unit 
needs as determined by the director: 
a. Thanksgiving 
b. Christmas 
c. New Year’s 
3. Full-time staff members will be required to work 24 hours of the following minor holidays, based on 
unit needs as determined by the director: 
a. Good Friday 
b. July 4th 
c. Labor Day 
4. Part-time staff members are required to work 12 hours of major and 12 hours of minor holiday time per 
year, based on unit needs as determined by the director. 
5. PRN staff members are required to work 12 hours of holiday time per year, based on unit needs as 
determined by the director.    
6. Holidays off will rotate every year for all staff. 
7. If a staff member calls in sick or is absent on a scheduled major holiday shift, the staff member will be 
required to make up that shift on the next major holiday at the director’s discretion. The same applies for 
minor holidays. 
8. All eligible team members are available to be pulled or cancelled during holidays and will be based on 
the needs of the Children’s Hospital. 
9. All of the above is at the discretion of the unit director and can be changed to fit the unit needs.
