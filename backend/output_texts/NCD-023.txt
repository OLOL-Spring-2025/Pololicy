Distribution:  Cardiovascular Lab 
 
Formulated By:  Cardiovascular Service Line 
 
Purpose: To provide guidelines for the management and archival of images. 
 
Applies To:  Cardiovascular Lab personnel  
 
Policy: An assigned Cardiovascular Interventional Technologist (CIT) will 
be responsible for the proper manipulation and archival of all 
images acquired in CVL. 
  
Procedure:   
 
1. Archive all cardiovascular images to Visage. 
2. Images can be viewed on Visage and/or the Epic record.
