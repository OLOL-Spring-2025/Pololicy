SCOPE: 
Applies to team members who work in or for Our Lady of the Lake Hospital, Inc. (OLOLH) retail/community 
pharmacies. 
PURPOSE: 
To state that OLOLH owned pharmacies do not provide financial incentives to staff members that are based on consumer 
utilization of services. 
POLICY: 
The pharmacy does not use financial incentives based directly on consumer utilization of services. [URAC PHARM 
Core 33  OLOLH expressly prohibits the use of inducements, kickbacks or other similar transactions tied to or 
associated with patient referrals. Employees and board members avoid conflict of interest issues by not accepting 
substantial gifts or any other remuneration from business associates. Financial incentives dependent on the outcome of a 
case are never accepted 
DEFINITIONS: 
Financial Incentives are monetary benefits offered to consumers, employees and organizations to encourage behavior or 
actions which otherwise would not take place. A financial incentive motivates actions which otherwise might not occur 
without the monetary benefit.
