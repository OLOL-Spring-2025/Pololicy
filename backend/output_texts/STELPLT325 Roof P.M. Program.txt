POLICY: 
  
Quarterly inspections performed on the roof by Plant Operations.  Corrective action will   
be taken as needed. 
  
SCOPE: 
  
            Applies to all Plant Operation mechanics to maintain the roof, increase  
            Roofâ€™s life and reduce energy consumption. 
  
PROCEDURE: 
  
            Quarterly Preventive Maintenance 
  
1.   Inspect metal flashing around the outside wall and vents.  All flashing should be sealed and in  
      good condition. 
2.   Inspect all drains for any debris that may clog the drain.   
3.   Inspect roof's condition.  Look for any open seams or splits. 
4.   Keep roof free of debris at all times 
5.    Make note of any standing water and make corrections as needed
