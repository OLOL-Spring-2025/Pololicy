PURPOSE: 
To assure proper collection of drug screen and breathe alcohol (evidential breath test) in accordance 
with company drug and alcohol policy or DOT RULE 49 CFR Part 40. 
POLICY: 
1. If an injured patient's company representative requests a drug screen or blood/breath alcohol, then: 
a. The company representative is given the phone/pager number of the Collection Company , Total 
Occupational Medical to call for their request.  
                         Day phone:  225-924-4460     After hours:   225-316-5606 (on call rep)    
b. The Collection Company Representative will come to the hospital, in a reasonable on -call 
response time to perform the collection of requested sample(s). 
c. All Chain of Custody forms, sample(s) collected, and testing results are handled by the 
Collection Company.
