PURPOSE:   
 
To define the accessibility of all organization medical records, including all OLOL physician clinics. 
 
POLICY:   
 
It is the policy of Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center to have all patient medical records 
accessible on a 24 hour per day basis. 
 
PROCEDURE:   
▪ During the hours of 8:00 a.m. through 5:00 p.m., Monday thru Friday, the clinics are open for 
business with medical records accessibility via the Office Managers. 
▪ From the hours of 5:00 p.m. through 8:00 a.m., any appropriate individual requesting a medical 
record must contact the Health Information Management Department or House Manager and provide 
the patient’s name, date of birth and social security number, if the number is known. 
➢ The HIM Staff or House Manager will contact the appropriate person at each clinic to obtain the 
requested information. 
▪ Should the record requested be located with our outside storage vendor, the vendor will be contacted 
and provided with the requested patient name and medical record number. The vendor will deliver 
the medical record to the Health Information Management Department or House Manager if the 
record is requested after hours.   
▪ The contact list for all OLOL clinics and physician practices is located in the HIM Department and 
in Patient Care Services.
