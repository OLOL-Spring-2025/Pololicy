SCOPE: 
Applies to team members who work in or for Our Lady of the Lake Hospital, Inc. (OLOLH) retail/community 
pharmacies. 
PURPOSE: 
This policy assures that infection control and waste management procedure are in place for the OLOLH 
retail/community pharmacies. 
POLICY: 
OLOLH pharmacies will seek to minimize transmission of any infectious disease from employee-to-employee or from 
employee-to-patient. 
DEFINITIONS: 
N/A 
PROCEDURE:  
 For employees who are diagnosed presumptively or confirmed to have a communicable disease, Policy EH1009 
will be followed. 
A. Pharmacy will stock face masks, gloves and hand sanitizer to facilitate infection control practices.  
 
B. OLOLH pharmacies will provide sharps containers for the safe disposal of broken vials, needles used in 
compounding, or other items that could potentially puncture skin. OLOLH pharmacies does not accept 
used needles or other biohazards from patients, but instead provides patients with free sharps containers 
and instructions for appropriate disposal of full sharps containers. [URAC P-PSC 1-2 (a-v)] [URAC 
PM 5-1 (a-vi)]

from, revise or eliminate this policy, at any time, in its sole discretion.
