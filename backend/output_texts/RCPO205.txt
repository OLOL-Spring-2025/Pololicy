POLICY: 
Family members will be permitted in the gym area to observe patient’s participation in therapy on a scheduled 
basis.  This may be requested by the family or treatment team. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
 
1. The family member or treatment team requests family observation in the treatment setting. 
2. The Case Manager discusses the purpose of the observation with the treatment team and family.  Observation 
time is agreed upon by all members involved. 
3. The family member attends therapy at the designated time and observes without disrupting the patient’s 
participation in therapy.
