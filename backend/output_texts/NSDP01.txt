The Nutritional Services Department of Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center is in 
compliance with the requirements of the Sanitary Code of the State of Louisiana.  The State 
conducts a yearly inspection.  The permit to operate is issued by the Department of Health 
and Human Resources, Office of Health Services and Environmental Quality.  The 
Nutritional Services Department is in compliance with local laws and regulations.  Local 
inspections are conducted periodically.  The Food Service Department is in compliance with 
federal regulations as required. The permits are posted in public view in the specific areas.  
Copies are kept on file in the food service accounts office.
