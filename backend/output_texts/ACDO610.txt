PURPOSE:  
This policy establishes guidelines to ensure the appropriate recording of all therapists daily, weekly, and  
monthly statistics.  
 
DEFINITIONS: 
None 
 
POLICY:  
It is the policy of the Acute Care Physical Medicine Department that therapists will record submit daily  
statistics of their time spent in patient and non patient chargeable care each day. The statistics will be  
recorded daily, weekly, and monthly and submitted to the finance office. 
 
PROCEDURES: 
1. Each therapist will record their daily statistics for patient chargeable and non-
patient chargeable care on the designated form, per departmentâ€™s Standard 
Operating Procedure. 
2. Each therapist shall place the completed form in the designated box before the 
end of the workday. 
3. Each morning, daily statistics sheets will be collected, totaled, and recorded in 
electronic records. Department totals will be submitted to the finance office.
