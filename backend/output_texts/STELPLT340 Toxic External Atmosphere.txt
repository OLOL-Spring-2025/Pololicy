Plant Operations 
  
Policy: 
  
To protect patients, staff and visitors from the effects of a potentially toxic external 
atmosphere that can include contamination by a chemical, cloud, smoke, or other such 
pollutants to the extent that it becomes a significant threat to life or health. 
  
Responsibility: 
  
Plant Operations 
  
Procedure: 
  
1.                  Notify the Administrative House Supervisor/ designee when the hospital is 
notified of a toxic external atmosphere. 
  
2.                  Contact Haz-Mat @ 9-911. 
  
3.                  Notify the Emergency Department personnel of toxic external atmosphere. Have 
all ambulance traffic and walk-in traffic from the outside redirected to the 
designated receiving area located at Entrance B. All doors from the Emergency   
             Department to the outside must be kept closed. 
  
4.                  Shut down all air handling units in the building, including outside air make-up  
wherever feasible. 
  
5.                  Direct the Security personnel / Plant Operations Department personnel to lock all 
entrances except the entrance to the “Entrance B” Lobby, the receiving area.

6.                    Request barricades and directional signs in the front of main entrances to 
redirect traffic to the correct receiving area. Assistance may be requested from the 
Ascension Parish Sheriff’s Office Dispatch @ 9-911 or 621-8300 
  
7.                    Request that the Administrative House Supervisor/ designee make an 
announcement via the public address system, requesting that no one leave the 
hospital or open outside doors until an “all clear” is given. 
  
8.                    Keep all interior doors leading to the receiving area closed.  Post signs 
restricting entrance by authorized personnel only.  The Plant Operations 
Department may dispatch officers to monitor these doors, if necessary.
