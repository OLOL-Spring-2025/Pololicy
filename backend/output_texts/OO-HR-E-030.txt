PURPOSE: 
The purpose of this policy is to describe the nature of employment at Our Lady of the Lake 
Regional Medical Center (OLOL). 
 
POLICY: 
A. Employment at OLOL is at will and may be terminated, for any reason, or no reason, by 
either party with or without cause at any time. 
 
B. No supervisor, manager or representative of OLOL, other than the Chief Executive Officer 
(CEO) of OLOL, has the authority to enter into any agreement for employment for any 
specified period or to make any promises or commitments contrary to the foregoing.  
 
C. Any employment agreement entered into by the CEO of OLOL is not enforceable unless in 
writing.
