PURPOSE: 
The purpose of this policy is to describe the Chaplain Services available to patients, families and employees 
at Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center (OLOL). 
 
POLICY: 
OLOL provides Chaplain Services to minister to the spiritual and emotional needs of patients and families, 
staff, physicians, volunteers, and OLOL itself.  Chaplains minister to people of all faith traditions. Patients 
and families may also receive ministry from their own religious representative whenever they desire.  
Chaplains serve as Witnesses and provide education on Advance Directives. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
1. Chaplain Service is available twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week for all patients and families. 
 
2. The chaplain may be contacted byone of the following methods: : 
a. Calling the Pastoral Care Office and leaving a message; 
b. Contacting the paging operator and having the Chaplain or Priest paged.  
c. Documenting a request for a Spiritual Care Screener or Consult in EPIC. 
 
3. Chaplains respond to all Stroke Alerts, Heart Alerts, Traumas and Codes.  Chaplains also respond to 
deaths when notified by nursing service.   
 
4. In the event of a Code Blue or Trauma, the communications operator will contact the 1st call chaplain as 
part of the response team.  The chaplain will respond within 10 minutes. If unable to do so, Chaplain 
will discuss suitable alternative with requesting party.
