PURPOSE: 
The purpose of this policy describes the Health Information Management policy on videotapes of patient 
care. 
 
POLICY: 
It is the policy of the Health Information Management Department of Our Lady of the Lake Regional 
Medical Center that videotapes of patient care will not be retained as part of the medical record. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
 
Videotapes of patient care procedures are educational in nature and belong to the physician.  Retention 
of videotapes are not the responsibility of the hospital, therefore, videotapes are not considered an 
official part of the legal medical record. 
 
Videotapes arriving in the Health Information Management Department will be forwarded to the 
physician performing the procedure.
