PURPOSE:  
This policy outlines the responsibilities of the Therapist for the Bedside/Critical Care Treatment 
Rotation. 
 
DEFINITIONS: 
None 
 
POLICY:  
It is the policy of the Acute Care Physical Medicine Department that a therapist assigned to a 
Bedside/Critical Care Rotation be responsible for the following responsibilities. 
 
 
PROCEDURES: 
 
1. Evaluate patients and make appropriate goals and treatments plans for each patient. 
2. Contact the physician to seek any appropriate orders or referrals needed. 
3. Responsible for appropriate treatment of each patient and supervision of assistants who treat these 
patients.  This includes progressing the patient as indicated. 
4. Check physician orders daily for new orders and check progress notes. If physician does not order 
specifically bedside, the patient can be brought to the gym when appropriate. 
5. Responsible for attending interdisciplinary discharge planning/staffing meetings as scheduled by the 
patient care unit. 
6. Each therapist is responsible for complying with current hospital approved team communication system, 
currently VOALTE   
7. Each therapist is responsible for acknowledging new orders when notified. 
8. Responsible for keeping daily treatment cards up to date with daily notes and charges. 
9. Responsible for entering charges and all required documentation near point of service. 
10. Responsible for documentation in chart when patient is placed on hold. 
11. Responsible for helping other areas when finished in your work area.
