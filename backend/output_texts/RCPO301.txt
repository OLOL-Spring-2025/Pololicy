PURPOSE: 
To maintain an objective evaluation of the success of the Rehab Center in meeting the needs of all persons served. 
POLICY: 
The Rehab Center will maintain a Program Evaluation system as a means to gather and analyze outcomes 
information in response to the needs of all stakeholders.  The analyzed information will be applied to improving 
performance in a variety of areas.  The Rehab Center will be responsible for sharing relevant information with 
stakeholders at a frequency that meets their needs.   
 PROCEDURE: 
1.) The Rehab Center Leadership will be responsible for compiling information to be used for Program 
Evaluation.   This information will contain the following information: 
 a.) Information on referrals, admissions, and discharges for all patients in the Rehab Center. 
 b.) Outcomes data on all patients admitted to and discharged from the Rehab Center.  
 c.) Financial analysis of including ALOS, expense reporting and reviews.  
 d.) Analysis of caseload descriptors which includes gender, age, race, diagnosis, referral sources, 
discharge characteristics, and payer sources on all patients. 
 e.) Overview of performance improvement status which reflects changes and improvements in 
departmental operations. 
 f.) Analysis of patient satisfaction survey results. 
2.)         Outcomes Data is collected via the UDSmr software.
