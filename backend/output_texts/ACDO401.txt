PURPOSE:  
This policy establishes guidelines for student affiliations which are established with universities and college that  
have PT, OT, ST, and Kinesiology Programs. 
 
 
DEFINITIONS: 
None 
 
POLICY:  
It is the policy of the Acute Care Physical Medicine Department to provide educational opportunities for 
student therapists or students who are seeking admission to therapy programs. 
 
PROCEDURES: 
 
1. Each student is assigned to a clinical supervisor who is responsible for supervising the student at all times. 
2. Each student affiliating in the Physical Medicine/Rehab Division will be responsible for following the 
policies and procedures of the department and the hospital. 
3. Academics has a current written contract on file for each university that utilizes our facility for clinical 
education purposes. 
4. Students are not permitted to use the Computer Documentation System until they demonstrate proficiency 
with regard to patient documentation. 
5. All absences must be made up at the discretion of the CCCE in accordance with university policy.
