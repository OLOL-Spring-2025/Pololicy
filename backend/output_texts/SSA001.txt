PURPOSE: 
To define level of service available to emergency and trauma patients. 
 
POLICY: 
The operating room (OR) will be staffed and equipped to allow prompt access to surgical and 
anesthesia service for emergency and/or trauma patients. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
1. The need for immediate admission to the OR will be determined by a surgeon. 
a. One Trauma room and Trauma team will be available 24/7. 
 
2. The OR will be staffed 24 hours a day. Specialty on-call teams are available. 
 
3. The OR maintains equipment, supplies, and instrumentation compatible for Level I Trauma 
certification.  
 
4. Once notification is received by the OR staff from the physician, the OR should be prepared 
immediately according to type of case/procedure to be done.  
 
5. When the trauma standby team is activated for a trauma procedure the following will occur: 
a. Request another available room and in-house team to fill dedicated trauma room.  
b. If no in-house team is available, then activate the call team and dedicate another 
trauma room.  
i. Call teams and trauma rooms are to be activated for every occurrence where 
a trauma will occupy the standby trauma team and room.

7. In an emergency situation, the OR staff will assist with arrangement for anesthesia coverage 
as directed by the surgeon. 
8. The post-op call room (or other dedicated block) will be used to ensure timely care of 
trauma ortho non-emergent patients.
