PURPOSE: 
To provide Patient Instructions for non-blood specimen collection to outpatients requiring laboratory testing 
 
Addresses: 
Venipuncture Charge Techs 
Venipuncture Phlebotomists 
 
POLICY: 
Based on physician lab orders the laboratory provides instructions to patients on how to properly collect and store 
urine and stool specimens for laboratory testing.  Below is a list of collection instructions the Venipuncture 
Department may be asked to provide outpatients.  For any questions regarding the collection instructions please 
contact the technical sections: 
• Clean Catch Mid-Stream Urine Sample: Patient Instructions (Hematology) 
• Infant Urine Collection: Patient Instructions (Hematology) 
• 24 Hour Urine Collection: Patient Instructions (Chemistry) 
• Stool Collection Adults: Patient Instructions (Microbiology) 
 
Copies of the above instructions are included with this policy. (Please see attached)
