PURPOSE: 
To provide a regulatory mechanism for allowing non-OR staff personal into the operating rooms 
during patient procedures. 
 
POLICY: 
A. Visitors 
a. Only those persons involved in the care of the patients or those granted observation 
privileges shall be allowed in the operating room. Examples: 
i. Students enrolled in an institution that has an agreement with Our Lady of 
the Lake (OLOL) go through Academic Affairs/GME for observation 
1. Nursing students 
2. Surgical technology 
3. Medical students 
ii. Associate or assistant of the physician 
iii. Person required to assist with instrumentation, equipment or supplies being 
utilized 
b. The following must obtain approval from the medical staff office, MSO for 
observation or scrub privileges if they are not members of the OLOL Medical-
Dental staff 
i. Physicians 
ii. PA 
iii. CRNA 
iv. Physician-sponsored observers 
B. Orientation 
a. All visitors must receive aseptic technique training coordinated by the Perioperative 
Educator 
b. Only visitors who have completed orientation will be allowed into the OR 
c. Need for additional orientation must be reassessed after one year of completing the 
aseptic technique class.
