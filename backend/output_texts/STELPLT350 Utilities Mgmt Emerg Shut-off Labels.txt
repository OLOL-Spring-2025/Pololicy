Applies to: 
  
Plant Operations 
  
  
Policy: 
  
To label clearly, and permanently emergency shut-off controls for the utility system 
components located throughout the facility, to include, but not limited to: 
  
            Natural Gas 
            Electricity 
            Water Distribution 
            Steam 
            Medical Air and Vacuum 
            Piped Gases 
  
Responsibility: 
  
Plant Operations 
  
Procedure: 
  
1.             The Plant Operations supervisor will make periodic rounds to ensure that all 
emergency shut-off labels are visible and in place. 
  
2.              Location and operation of the controls for complete or partial shut-off will be in-
serviced to all Plant Operations Department personnel during orientation and 
annually thereafter.
