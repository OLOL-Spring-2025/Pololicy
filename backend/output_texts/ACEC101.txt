PURPOSE:  
This policy establishes guidelines to ensure that wheelchairs are regularly cleaned and maintained and that all  
parts are accounted for after patient use. 
 
 
DEFINITIONS: 
None 
 
POLICY:  
It is the policy of the Acute Care Physical Medicine Department that wheelchairs will be cleaned and  
maintained for patient safety in Acute Care Physical Medicine and Acute Care  
Physical Medicine Equipment rooms. 
 
 
PROCEDURES: 
1.     A sheet or towel is placed in the seat of the chair before the patient is transferred into the chair. 
2.     The sheet or towel is replaced with a new sheet or towel with each patient. 
3.   Wheelchairs are cleaned with hospital designated cleaning cloths after each patient use, at the end of the   
  day, and when visibly soiled. 
4.  All needed repairs to wheelchairs should be reported to the manager of Acute Physical Medicine. 
5.  Wheelchairs that need service/repaired are scheduled to be repaired with the appropriate vendor.
