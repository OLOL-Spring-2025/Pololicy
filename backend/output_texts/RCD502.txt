POLICY: Case Managers will complete a discharge summary on each patient discharged from the 
Rehabilitation Unit.  The summaries will address the recommended referral action.  The 
discharge summary will also address the recommendations of the team to assist the patient in 
maintaining and/or improving post-discharge function and increased independence.
