SCOPE: 
Applies to team members who work in or for Our Lady of the Lake Hospital, Inc. (OLOLH) pharmacies. 
PURPOSE: 
This policy provides information on the pharmacy’s participation in clinical research. 
POLICY: 
This policy specifies how the pharmacy, its employees and patients will participate in the provision of drug therapies that 
are experimental, not-yet FDA approved or investigational. 
 
DEFINITIONS: 
N/A 
PROCEDURE: 
A. For provision of each specific therapeutic agent, the pharmacy manager shall directly develop or 
supervise development of procedural protocols that address the following: 
1. Informing clients/patients of their responsibilities 
2. Informing clients/patients of their right to refuse investigational drugs or experimental therapies 
3. Informing clients/patients of their right to refuse to participate in research and clinical studies 
4. Notifying clients/patients that they will not be discriminated against for refusal to participate in 
research and clinical studies 
5. Stating which personnel can administer investigational medications/treatments 
6. Describing personnel’s role in monitoring a patient’s response to investigational 
medications/treatments and related services and how those personnel have been trained for 
participation 
7. Identifying the responsibility for obtaining informed consent 
8. Definitions, procedures or policies specific to each regimen 
9. Defining the use of experimental and investigational drugs and other atypical treatments and 
interventions 
 
B. At the time of approval of this Policy and Procedure, the pharmacy has no involvement in dispensing 
investigational or experimental therapies.
