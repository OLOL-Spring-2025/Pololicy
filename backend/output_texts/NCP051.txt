Title: Critical Care Ventilator Management 
Policy 
Reference #: NCP-051 
Supersedes #:  
Date of Origination: March 26, 2007 Review Date:    March 4, 2024  
Revision Date: June 11, 2012 
 
 
PURPOSE: 
To ensure consistent care of the patient in the adult ICU on a ventilator. 
 
POLICY:  
1. Upon admission to the adult ICU, patients on ventilators will have the Critical Care Ventilator 
Management order set initiated. 
 
2. An order is to be obtained from the physician prior to initiating the critical care ventilator order 
set. 
 
3. If cardiac surgery patients require mechanical ventilation greater than 24hours, the order set shall 
be initiated by an order from the physician. 
 
4.  The critical care ventilator order set must be discontinued upon extubation.
