POLICY: 
  
            There are readings taken and operational checks made daily.  Monthly and annual  
            servicing is performed on all pneumatic compressors. 
  
SCOPE: 
  
            Applies to all Plant Operations employees so to maintain equipment efficiency and 
            to prevent sudden breakdowns. 
  
PROCEDURE: 
  
            Daily Preventative Maintenance 
  
            1.  Visual check for air or oil  
            2.  Record oil level and pressure. 
              
            Monthly Preventative Maintenance 
  
            1. Check overall operations. 
            2. Check belt tension and alignment.  Adjust as needed or replace. 
            3. Check air filter, replace as needed. 
            4. Lubricate areas as needed. 
            5. Record starting/stopping pressures. 
            6. Read motor ampere.  All leads should read close to the same.  Take corrective action  
    as needed. 
            7.  Correct all air or oil leaks. 
            8.  Clean equipment. UNITS MUST BE KEPT CLEAN!

Annually Preventative Maintenance 
  
            1.  Replace crankcase oil motor. 
            2.  Inspect starter contacts for wear.  CLEAN AS NEEDED 
            3.  Check for loose connections in the plumbing and electrical systems and correct. 
            4.  Repaint any other accessories if paint is chipping or corrosion is found.
