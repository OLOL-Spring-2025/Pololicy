PURPOSE: 
To state admit/transfer/discharge criteria for the Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU). 
I. TRANSFER CRITERIA: 
A. NICU patients may be transferred to the Universal Care Unit (UCU), PICU or other in-patient 
unit based on clinical needs, acuity and/or per Neonatologist/ provider discretion. 
B. If a neonate comes through the ER and requires surgery prior to being admitted to the NICU, the 
ER physician will notify the Neonatologist of the pending admit. Once surgery is completed on 
admit/transfer order for bed placement will be entered into EPIC. The Anesthesiologist will 
contact the Neonatologist/provider to give patient handoff prior to bringing the patient to the 
unit. The Circulator will give patient handoff to the NICU nurse. 
II.  DISCHARGE CRITERIA: 
A. Multidisciplinary rounds will be conducted weekly to assess readiness for discharge.  
B. All disciplines will work with families to get necessary equipment, medications, teaching, setting 
follow up appointments completed prior to discharge. 
C. All discharge teaching will be documented in electronic medical record (EMR).
