Policy: 
  
In the event of a malfunction and/or failure of the fire alarm system, the following 
procedure will be followed.  
  
Responsibility: 
  
Plant Operations 
  
Procedure: 
  
1.                  Notify the local Gonzales Fire Department. 
2.                  Notify the Manager of Facilities/and or Supervisor. 
3.                  Notify Johnson Controls (fire alarm company) @ 1-800-456-5787. 
4.                  Manager of Facilities will post the fire watch. 
5.                  Notify the Administrative House Supervisor and all departments of the 
              anticipated down time. 
6.                  As soon as possible, inform the local Gonzales Fire Department, the fire alarm   
              company, the administrative supervisor “on call” and all departments when the    
              repairs have been completed. 
7.                  Check with the fire alarm monitoring company, Johnson Controls, @ 1-888-746-   
             7539 to ensure that the fire alarm signal is being received from our facility.  
    Hospital:  Acct #211-4119 
 Med Plaza 1:  Acct#211-4121 
    Med Plaza II:  Acct #211-4120 
8.    Discontinue the fire watch.
