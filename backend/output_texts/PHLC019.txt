DEXAMETHASONE SUPPRESSION TEST COLLECTION 
       Date Issue  Date Effective Date

PURPOSE: 
To provide for the proper collection of Dexamethasone Suppression test specimens 
 
Addresses: 
Supervisors 
Venipuncture Charge Techs 
Venipuncture Phlebotomists 
 
POLICY: 
A. Venipuncture personnel participate in the drawing patients for dexamethasone suppression test 
B. The test may not begin until nursing personnel have the required medication on the nursing unit 
C. Patients are to be drawn in a timely manner 
D. Draw priority is to be given to patients undergoing Dexamethasone Suppression testing 
PROCEDURE: 
A. When the lab order is received to perform Dexamethasone testing, the charge tech is to verify with nursing 
personnel that: 
1. A correct order was placed 
2. The patient’s nurse has the medication on the floor before drawing the baseline 
B. Specimens are drawn in SST or Red top tubes 
C. Baseline cortisol level is drawn at 22:45 the day of testing 
D. The nurse gives the patient the loading dose of dexamethasone following the drawing of the baseline level 
E. The patient is then drawn the next day at these specified times: 
1. 08:00 
2. 16:00 
3. 23:00 
Limitations of Procedure: 
• Verify that the required drug is on the unit before drawing the baseline level 
• Clearly document each draw time on the laboratory label
