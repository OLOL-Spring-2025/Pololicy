PURPOSE:  
This policy ensures that all employees receive orientation to the Physical Medicine/Rehabilitation department 
upon initial employment. 
 
 
DEFINITIONS: 
None 
 
 
POLICY:  
It is the policy of the Acute Care Physical Medicine Department that every employee receives a 
department specific and job specific orientation. 
 
 
PROCEDURES: 
 
1. The department manager will ensure that all new employees are scheduled to receive departmental orientation 
upon initial employment. 
2. The manager, or other designated staff persons, will review all topics addressed on the orientation checklist 
with the new employee during the employeeâ€™s orientation period (usually within first four weeks of 
employment). 
3. Each employee should be oriented to the department as well as all disciplines housed within the department 
during the first month of employment.  
4. Once orientation is complete, all checklists must be signed and dated by the orientation preceptor and given to 
the manager to keep in the employee folder.
