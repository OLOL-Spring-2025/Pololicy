PURPOSE: 
To define areas in which anesthesia may be administered. 
 
POLICY: 
Anesthesia should be administered by Anesthesiologist or licensed Anesthetist in the Operating 
Room Post Anesthesia Care Unit (PACU), Endoscopy Center, Critical Care Unit, Cardiovascular 
and Imaging Services areas.  
 
PROCEDURE: 
1.         Appropriate anesthesia equipment, supplies, medications, trained staff, and other items (i.e. 
blood products) that are identified as essential must be available prior to administration of 
anesthesia. 
 
2.         The nursing staff will assist the Anesthesiologist or CRNA, as required and within scope of 
practice, while anesthesia care is being started, maintained, or completed.  
 
3.         An anesthesia consent and operative anesthesia record must be completed as required by the 
procedure. 
 
4.         At the completion of the procedure, the patient will be transferred to the PACU or 
appropriate nursing unit, according to type of anesthesia administered.
