Title: Plumbing Systems  
Revision Date: 02/05/20 
Review Date: 07/01/15 
Reference # PS024 Origin Date: September 1988 
 
PURPOSE: 
To provide a safe physical environment for patients, staff and visitors at all Our Lady of the Lake 
Regional Medical Center (OLOL) network locations. 
 
POLICY: 
All plumbing systems at OLOL are designed, installed, and maintained to provide complete and safe 
supply and wastewater removal. Systems are designed by professional engineers and installed by a 
licensed plumbing contractor. Alterations to such systems shall be likewise. Potable water is supplied by 
a municipal water system. 
 
PROCEDURES: 
1. Systems shall be maintained as designed and installed to provide maximum efficiency and safe 
utilization. Safety components such as vacuum breakers shall never be removed from a system. 
2. Vacuum breakers must be utilized at all hose bibs. 
3. Aerator devices on faucets shall not be used in an effort to reduce points of bacteria growth. 
4. Wastewater systems shall be maintained to peak efficiency. 
4.   
5. Back flow siphonage devices (preventers) are installed in the domestic water supply. for the 
following applications: 
a. Boiler plant equipment 
b. Laundry 
c. Dietary 
d. Laboratory 
e. Designated equipment areas
