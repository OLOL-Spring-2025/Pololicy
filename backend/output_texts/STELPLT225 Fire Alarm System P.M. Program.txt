POLICY: 
  
There are tests and inspections performed on the Fire Alarm System & Sprinkler System.  
Corrective action will be taken as needed. 
  
SCOPE: 
  
Applies to all Plant Operations team members to maintain the Fire Alarm & Sprinkler 
Systems.  With the use of this program patients, employees and medical staff will be 
safely housed at St. Elizabeth Hospital. 
  
PROCEDURE: 
  
        Smoke and fire dampers tested every 6 years 
Standpipe flow test every five years 
Duct, heat, smoke detectors, pull boxes, electrical releasing devices tested annually 
Notification devices annually 
Main drain tests at system low point or at all system risers annually 
Gaseous extinguishing systems inspected annually 
Portable fire extinguishers maintained annually 
Smoke detection shutdown devices for HVAC tested annually 
Valve tamper switches tested semiannually 
Kitchen suppression semi-annual testing 
Water flow devices inspected quarterly 
Emergency services notification transmission equipment tested quarterly 
Fire department connections inspected quarterly 
Portable fire extinguishers inspected monthly
