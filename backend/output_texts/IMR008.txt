PURPOSE: 
To outline the immediate steps that must be taken to provide prompt medical care to the patient, 
as well as insure the safety of all responding personnel and protect the magnet, in the event of  a 
medical emergency during and MRI examination. 
POLICY: 
A. Initiate a Code Blue or MET Team.  
B. Any radiology personnel in the immediate area should be summoned to provide 
assistance, and the magnet room door shall be closed and locked to prevent anyone from 
entering the magnet room without proper screening for contraindications to MRI or 
possession of loose magnetic objects.  
C. The patient shall be transferred to the non-magnetic patient cart, or transported out of the 
room on the dockable table where applicable.  
D. The patient shall then be transported outside of MRI Zone 3 where a crash cart and 
oxygen are located. 
E. The magnet room door shall be closed and locked after the patient and all personnel have 
left the room. The control and adjacent room doors should also be closed and locked to 
prevent any responding personnel from entering the restricted magnetic field area without 
proper screening by MRI personnel. 
Addendum: 
The above policy applies to OLOL Livingston Campus as well. 
For locations that are not on main OLOLRMC campus, please refer to the Offsite Emergency Policy
