POLICY: 
It is the policy of the Rehabilitation Center to ensure that overnight visitation enhances the recovery of each 
patient and does not detract from the therapeutic efforts and goals of the treatment plan. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
Patients in semi-private rooms will be allowed to have one person of the same sex as the patient stay overnight 
only when the physician deems this medically necessary. 
 
Patients in private rooms will be allowed to have one person stay overnight only when deemed medically 
necessary by the physician.
