Purpose: 
 
To establish guidelines for the patient referral from the Cardiopulmonary Rehabilitation program to other 
hospital programs. 
 
Applies To:                      All Cardiopulmonary Rehabilitation Staff 
 
Policy: 
 
All patients are offered options for more individual and in -depth care, and when appropriate, a patient 
may be referred for educational or emotional reasons to other programs offered by Our Lady of the Lake 
or another facility for the purpose of enhancing patient care. Available programs include: 
 
Social Services- Individuals and/or families may receive a session with the opportunity to continue, if 
desired. 
 
Nutritional Services- Individuals and /or families may be referred to the outpatient dietitian for additional 
  individual diet instruction, if desired. 
  
 
Procedure: 
 
The patient and family are informed during orientation concerning additional programs offered by Our 
Lady of the Lake and how to access those programs, if desired. 
 
The patient or family will notify Cardiopulmonary Rehabilitation staff of desire to participate in desired 
program. The Cardiopulmonary Rehabilitation staff member will then contact the department 
administering the desired program and follow established procedure to enter patient in that program. 
 
Considering the patientâ€™s risk factors, emotional state, or family dynamics, Cardiopulmonary 
Rehabilitation may suggest and encourage that the patient and/or family participate in any of the 
additional programs offered by Our Lady of the Lake. 
 
Patients and/or family will only be referred to additional hospital programs when the patient and/or family 
either requests or agrees to be referred to that program.
