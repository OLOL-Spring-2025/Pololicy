Policy: 
  
In the event of a failure of the medical air system or the medical vacuum system the 
following procedures will be followed. 
  
Responsibility: 
  
Plant Operations 
  
Procedure: 
  
Medical Air System: 
  
1.                  Check the compressors to see if they are functioning properly.  If one compressor    
              has failed, switch valves and isolate the defective unit.   
2.                  Check the filters to make certain that they are not plugged. 
3.                  Check the dryers to be sure they are functioning properly. 
4.                  In the event that the main supply line has ruptured, request emergency outside     
              assistance from one of the vendors (Preventive Maintenance for Gulf Coast, 225-  
              806-0785)  on the “Utility Emergency List” located in Plant Ops. 
5.                   If a total loss of medical air has occurred, notify the following:   
  
Respiratory Therapy Department 
Administrative House Supervisor “on call”/designee  
Plant Operations Director along with supervisor/designee

6.                  The Plant Operations supervisor shall be responsible for ordering additional 
Medical Air supplies until the failure has been corrected, and purity tests made (if 
required). 
  
  
Medical Vacuum System: 
  
1.                  Check the vacuum pumps to be sure they are functioning properly. 
2.                  Check all valves; be sure they are in the proper position. 
3.                  Check for disruption in the supply line (pumps and valves are functioning   
              properly). 
4.                  If a pump failure has occurred in the vacuum system, notify the Manager of 
Facilities, along with the Supervisor, Administrative House Supervisor/designee,   
             ICU Supervisor, and the Supervisor of Surgery.   
5.                  Notify the effected departments as to the length of time required to make repairs.  
6.                  If the repairs are beyond the scope of the Plant Operations department call one of    
             the vendors on the “Utility Emergency List” located in Plant Ops.
