PURPOSE:  
This policy establishes guidelines to ensure appropriate charging method for skilled therapeutic services. 
 
 
DEFINITIONS: 
None 
 
POLICY:  
It is the policy of the Acute Care Physical Medicine Department that therapists will submit daily patient 
charges that supports the rendering of skilled therapeutic services. 
 
PROCEDURES: 
Responsibilities of the Therapist 
 
1. Each therapist will enter charges online in the computer system on day of service for each patient. 
2. If the Computer system is down, Charges will be included on the department’s downtime therapy form to be 
entered when the system is up. 
3. Therapists are to enter charges for procedures based on 15 minute timed increments unless otherwise 
specified.  Those charges that are not time-based are designated as such on the charge sheet and should only 
be entered as one (1) unit per 15 minutes for the visit.  The timed increments are as follows: 
a. 8-22 minutes = 1 unit 
b. 23-37 minutes = 2 units 
c. 38-52 minutes = 3 units 
d. 53-67 minutes = 4 units 
e. 68-82 minutes = 5 units 
4. The overall charge will consist of PT, OT, and ST charges based on appropriate CPT coding and recorded 
daily on the department’s designated statistics/charge sheet.
