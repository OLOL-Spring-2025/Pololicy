PURPOSE:  
The glucose 2 hour post prandial test measures blood glucose exactly 2 hours after a meal or glucose 
solution is consumed.  The 2 hour post prandial glucose test may be used to help diagnose or follow 
individuals with impaired glucose tolerance or diabetes mellitus.   
 
Addresses: 
Phlebotomist 
Phlebotomist Sr. 
Medical Laboratory Technicians 
Medical Technologist 
 
PROCEDURE: 
1. Test best performed in early morning 
2. Ensure the patient has been fasting at least 8-12 hours 
3. Have patient consume an adequate meal within a 15-20-minute time frame.  
Alternative: glucose solution (100 g.) allowing 5 minutes for consumption. 
4. Collect blood in a gray tube or SST tube exactly 2 hours after the patient has finished the meal or 
glucose solution. 
5. Label specimen tubes and send specimens to the Central Processing Department for processing
