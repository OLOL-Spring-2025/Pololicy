PURPOSE: 
The purpose of this policy is  to outline the Resident / Fellow Agreement of Appointment required of 
all residents upon selection to or promotion to the next program year level within an OLOL  
sponsored residency program.  
 
POLICY: 
A. In compliance with the ACGME Institutional Requirements, Re sident/ Fellow  are provided 
with a written Agreement of Appointment contract outlining the terms and conditions of their 
appointment to a program. The Sponsoring Institution must monitor each of its programs 
regarding implementation of terms and conditions of appointment.  
 
B. The Resident Agreement of Appointment must contain or provide reference to the 
 following: 
• Resident/ Fellow responsibilities 
• Duration of appointment 
• Financial support 
• Conditions for reappointment and promotion to a subsequent PGY level 
• Grievance and due process 
• Professional liability insurance 
• Health insurance benefits 
• Disability insurance 
• Vacation and leave(s) of absence for residents/fellows, including medical, parental, and 
caregiver leave(s) of absence  Timely notice of the effect of leave(s) on the ability of 
residents to satisfy requirements for program completion. 
• Information related to eligibility for specialty board examinations. 
• Institutional policies and  procedures regarding resident/fellow clinical and educational 
work hours and moonlighting 
• Other pertinent policy, procedures, rules and regulations  GME/ADM/05 
 
C. The GMEC will conduct an annual review of the Resident Agreement of Appointment and 
 will make recommendations to administration regarding revisions to the agreement as well as 
resident stipends and benefits.
