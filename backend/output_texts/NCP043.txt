Title: Drawing Blood Samples from Arterial Lines

PURPOSE: 
To provide a means for obtaining blood samples from arterial lines. 
 
POLICY: 
A. Respiratory therapist and registered nurses, with appropriate knowledge and skills, may draw blood 
samples from arterial lines.  
 
B. Aseptic technique and Standard Precautions will be observed throughout the procedure. 
 
C. Sample will be drawn using the current online reference for blood sampling from an indwelling 
arterial catheter (closed loop system).
