PURPOSE: 
To create a safe environment when giving high alert medications to pediatric patient. 
POLICY: 
The following medications require two licensed nurses to perform a double check prior to administration.  
 
Desmopressin (DDAVP) 
Digoxin 
Insulin 
Concentrated IV electrolytes (i.e., potassium, calcium, magnesium) 
TPN/Lipids 
Chemotherapy 
TPA, excluding doses utilized for line occlusion 
Antithrombotics, excluding prefilled heparin syringes 
            * Includes Continuous IV heparin nomogram and high dose (1000 unit/mL) heparin 
 
For continuous infusions (see list below), medications are to be checked by two licensed nurses: 
prior to administration/initiation  
concentration changes 
with a new pump set up for the same infusion,  
change of caregiver. 
 
Epinephrine  
Inotropic infusions 
      Continuous Narcotic Infusions which include PCA and epidural infusions 
      Continuous IV sedative Infusions
