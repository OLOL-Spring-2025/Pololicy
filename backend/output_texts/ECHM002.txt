POLICY: 
Any department which stores or uses materials/products considered "Hazardous," in terms of the chemicals contained in 
them, shall have access to the Safety Data Sheet (SDS) for the product.  
 
PROCEDURE: 
 Safety Data Sheets (SDS) are available to all employees through the SDS Online E-Binder located on TeamLink.  
 
DEPARTMENT DIRECTOR RESPONSIBILITIES: 
The personnel of the department shall be oriented to SDS Online E-Binder including: 
• Existence of the E-Binder and how to access it. 
• How to read and interpret a Safety Data Sheet. (See Policy and Procedure on Reading and Interpreting Safety Data 
Sheets). 
• All departments using materials considered "Hazardous" and have a Hazardous Materials Notebook in the department 
for reference.
