POLICY: 
 
Medications shall be packaged and dispensed in containers that: 
 
• Meet legal requirements. 
• Ensure the integrity of the medications. 
• Where applicable or required- protect the medications from light, moisture and air. 
 
TYPES OF CONTAINERS 
 
Containers shall be suitable for the type and amount of medication dispensed.   
 
Prepackaged medications or medications in manufacturer's original containers shall be dispensed when 
possible.  Disposable containers shall not be reused and shall be disposed of in accordance with the 
facility's policy. 
 
CHILD-RESISTANT DISPENSING CONTAINERS 
 
Medications dispensed to ambulatory -care patients may be dispensed in child resistant- containers with 
the following exceptions: 
 
• The medication is specifically exempt. 
• The patient specifically requests a nonresistant container. 
• The prescriber specifically authorizes a nonresistant container. 
 
The contents of a child resistant- package may not be transferred to a non-resistant container.
