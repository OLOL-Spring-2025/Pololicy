Title: Fire Warning / Safety Devices   
Revision Date: 02/05/2020; 11/01/2017 
Review Date: 09/30/2021; 12/11/2020 
Reference # PS007 Origin Date: October 1988 
 
POLICY: 
The fire warning and safety systems at Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center (OLOL) and all 
locations where OLOL operates shall be designed, installed, and maintained according to all applicable 
codes and standards for the purpose of protection to patients, personnel, visitors and property from fire 
and the products of combustion. 
 
PROCEDURES: 
1. The fire warning system shall be electrically supervised and manually operated. 
2. Manual fire alarm boxes shall be located in the path of normal exit, be readily accessible, and remain 
unobstructed at all times. 
3. Visual signal devices shall be distinctive form other signals used in the areas and be audible over 
operational noise in the area. 
4. The fire alarm system shall have automatic direct connection to a UL listed monitoring company for 
the purpose of notification to fire department. 
5. Activation of the fire alarm system shall automatically shut down air handling systems, close smoke 
dampers and provide for the release and closure of all smoke and fire rated doors.
