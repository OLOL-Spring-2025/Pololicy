PURPOSE:  
 
To provide guidelines for the provision of academic education to children and adolescents who are 
inpatients at Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center (OLOLRMC). 
 
POLICY: 
 
OLOL will facilitate the provision of academic education to those children and adolescents who are 
inpatients at Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center, when appropriate.   
  
PROCEDURE:   
 
1. On the Mental and Behavior Health Unit time is allotted for educational studies as part of the 
treatment schedule on the adolescent unit. 
 
2. For inpatients, the Social Worker will be notified of any school age patient that reach es a hospital 
stay of 3  days to discuss educational requirements with the patient’s family/guardian. The patient’s  
parent/guardian must notify the school of the absence and make arrangements to gather the 
assignments for the patient to complete and return.  If the patient’s stay extends to 7 days, the Social 
Worker, along with the child’s physician and members of the nursing staff, will make 
recommendations regarding the patient’s length of stay and educational requirements. The parents 
will contact the school and make the necessary arrangements to coordinate the child’s continued 
academic education until the child is discharged or until it is determined that it is no longer 
appropriate.
