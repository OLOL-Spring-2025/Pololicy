PURPOSE 
The purpose of this policy is to describe the specific process regarding stolen property, thefts and pilferage in reference to 
patients, visitors and Team Members on the premises of a property owned or operated by Our Lady of the Lake Regional 
Medical Center. 
POLICY 
To discourage visitors, patients and Team Members from taking property not belonging to them, Our Lady of the Lake 
Regional Medical Center will prosecute anyone found taking items for personal use that belong to the Medical Center. 
Any employee who is found taking a patient's, visitorâ€™s or other Team Members' property without permission is subject to 
immediate termination of employment, and will be reported to Law Enforcement . Visitors and patients found taking 
items will also be reported to Law Enforcement. 
PROCEDURE 
If any person becomes aware that another person (visitor, patient, or Team Member) is stealing at the Medical Center, he 
or she should immediately contact Administration or Security. Any report of stolen property will be investigated. The 
authorities will be contacted if needed to assist in the investigation.  
If a person is found stealing Medical Center property, the Medical Center will file charges against the person. If a Team 
Member is found stealing Medical Center or other property, follow the procedures in policy OrgOps/HR/P/002 "Team 
Member Coaching and Progressive Discipline" for termination of the employee. The Medical Center will assist in the 
investigation for theft of any other property.
