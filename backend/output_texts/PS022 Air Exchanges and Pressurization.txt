Title: Air Exchanges and Pressurization 
Revision Date: 12/22/20 
Review Date: 02/05/20 
Reference # PS022 Origin Date: August 1988 
 
PURPOSE: 
To provide a safe physical environment for patients, staff and visitors at all Our Lady of the Lake 
Regional Medical Center (OLOL) network locations. 
 
POLICY: 
Our Lady of the Lake will evaluate air exchanges per hour and positive/negative pressurization in areas 
identified by ASHRAE 170 and the Ventilation Risk Assessment. 
 
PROCEDURES: 
1. Continuous monitoring of areas identified as high and medium risk achieved by ball-in-the-wall, 
digital, and Trane Building Automation Systems (BAS).  Areas identified as low risk are 
periodically evaluated. 
2. A qualified vendor will test the HVAC system semi-annually.  
3. The test report will be reviewed for conformance to ASHRAE 170 regulating air exchanges, 
pressure relationships, humidity, and temperature.  Failure to meet standards shall be rectified 
immediately. 
4. Copies of the test reports will be retained in Plant Services for comparison with previous and future 
testing.
