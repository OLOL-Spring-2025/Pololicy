PURPOSE: 
To provide instructional references for the safe operation of equipment used in the Endoscopy. 
 
POLICY: 
Equipment manuals are available and accessible to staff at all times. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
 
1. Equipment manuals will be listed and referenced by common and proper names in the 
Equipment Manual File. 
 
2. As new pieces of equipment are acquired, operational manuals will be added.  
 
3.  The Equipment Manual File will be reviewed and revised annually for additions and 
 deletions by the Nurse Manager or designee. 
 
4.  If any equipment malfunctions: 
a. Notify the Charge Nurse, Nurse Manager or Supervisor 
b. Save all supplies and wrappers 
c. Impound equipment in Risk Management Department 
d. Complete a Patient Safety /Event Report and Chain of Custody form.  Turn both 
forms into Risk Management. 
e. Tag equipment for risk management
