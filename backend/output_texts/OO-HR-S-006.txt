PURPOSE 
The purpose of this policy is to define the process for changing an individualâ€™s employment status.  
These changes may include a modification in an employee's position title, status, pay rate, date of 
termination, or period of leave of absence. 
 
POLICY 
Formal approval is required from the Department Head and the appropriate Human Resource 
Representative for all changes to an individual's employment status. 
 
PROCEDURES 
1. The Department Head is responsible for initiating a personnel action for: 
a. Position change/promotion 
b. Pay rate change 
c. Status change 
d. Leave of absence 
e. Voluntary Termination 
f. Transfer 
 
2. Human Resources will initiate documentation and/or personnel action in the following situations: 
a. New Hires 
b. Re-Hires 
c. Involuntary Terminations 
 
3. All changes in an individual's employment are subject to the approval of the responsible Department 
Head and Human Resources.
