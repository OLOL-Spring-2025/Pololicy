PURPOSE:  
This policy establishes guidelines to ensure that the splint pan remains clean, thus decreasing the possibility of 
infection. 
 
DEFINITIONS: 
None 
 
POLICY:  
It is the policy of the Acute Care Physical Medicine Department that the Splint Pan will be cleaned weekly. 
 
 
PROCEDURES: 
1.         Turn splint pan off and let cool. 
2. Open the valve and drain the water into a bucket or floor drain.  
3. Disinfect with hospital approved product (following cleaning per productâ€™s manufacturer 
guidelines/instructions). 
4. Close valve and refill with water. 
5. Turn the splint pan back on.
