COFFEE SERVICE FOR GENERAL SURGERY 
AND CRITICAL CARE WAITING ROOMS 
 
 
The Nutritional Services Department in conjunction with the Critical Care Department 
provides complimentary coffee to relatives and friends of patients in Surgery and Critical 
Care Units.  This service is provided seven days per week. 
 
PROCEDURE 
 
    1. The Nutritional Services Department will be responsible for obtaining a coffee 
maker for both the General Surgery Waiting Area and the Critical Care Waiting 
Area. 
 
    2. Coffee ingredients, stirrers, creamers and sugar are requisitioned by the critical care 
waiting room attendants from the Nutritional Service Storeroom. 
            
    3. The Critical Care Waiting Room Attendant will assume the responsibility to pick up 
the supplies and deliver them to the appropriate area.  The Attendant Clerk will also 
assume responsibility for the preparation of all coffee and the cleanliness and 
sanitation of the area in the waiting rooms. 
     
4. The Nutritional Services Department is responsible for maintaining an adequate 
stock of supplies.  The Nutritional Services Department is also responsible for 
calling the company if the machine is not functioning properly.
