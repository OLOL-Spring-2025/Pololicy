PURPOSE 
The purpose of this policy is to ensure uniform procedures and policies for the loan of equipment and 
supplies to ensure that assets of the hospital are appropriately protected. 
 
POLICY 
Our Lady of the Lake Regional Medical Center (OLOLRMC) may lend or borrow supplies and/or 
equipment to and from other health care institutions if there is a special need or benefit to patients. Such 
borrowing/lending must be kept to a minimum and should not become a substitute for adequate 
inventories or planning, nor should OLOL extend such borrowing/lending to other institutions as to 
become a distribution point to these facilities.   
 
PROCEDURES 
1. Requests for supplies and equipment should go through the Materials Management Department or 
the Administrator on Call. 
 
2. If a decision is made to loan/borrow any item, the requesting hospital must: 
 
a. Provide transportation both to and from the lender. 
 
b. Agree to repair or replace equipment in case of damage or loss. 
 
c. Sign a loan slip upon receipt of the supplies and/or equipment.   
 
3. The loan slip for equipment and/or supplies will be forwarded to the Materials Management 
Department. 
 
4. When the loaned/borrowed item is returned, the receipt of same will be noted to reflect the time, date 
and any charges.
