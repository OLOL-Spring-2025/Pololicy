POLICY: 
Stock will always be rotated so that the older stock is utilized first.  All consumables will 
be stored as recommended by the manufacturer.  The Sensor Cassettes and Solution 
Packs are properly labeled with contents, storage requirements, and expiration dates. The 
system is designed to prevent the utilization of any expired Sensor Cassette or Solution 
Pack.  The sensor cassettes require refrigeration upon arrival. The Sensor Cassettes are 
stored at a temperature of 2-8°C. The Solution Packs do not require refrigeration. The 
Solution Packs are stored at a temperature of 2-25°C and a storage humidity of 20-80%. 
The ABL90 Flex analyzers internally read the expiration date of the Sensor Cassette and 
Solution Pack and will not allow their use after the expiration date. All heparinized 
syringes are used within the expiration date and are stored per the manufacturer 
instruction or will be discarded.
