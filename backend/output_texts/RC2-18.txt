PURPOSE:  
To identify the organizational chart for the Respiratory Care Department at each Our Lady of the 
Lake site. The leadership provided for clinical practice and laboratory direction is identified and 
defined below.  
 
DEFINITIONS: 
Medical Director of Respiratory Care Department: 
Respiratory Therapy services will be directed and overseen by the Medical Director of 
Respiratory Care, as designated in this policy at each site.  The Medical Director must approve 
all policy changes within the Respiratory Care Department as it relates to clinical practice. This 
supervision will remain in accordance with licensure specifications mandated by the Louisiana 
Respiratory Therapy Practice Act. All respiratory therapists will practice under the supervision 
of the Medical Director of Respiratory Care in accordance with the LSBME. 
 
Laboratory Director of the Blood Gas Laboratory: 
The Laboratory Director will oversee the Blood Gas Laboratories, as designated below at 
each site. The Laboratory Director will ensure all policies and procedures are 
implemented and are in compliance with the standards and laws set by local, state or 
federal regulatory agencies.  
 
Administratively, Respiratory Care reports to the Vice President of Operations.  
 
All positions within the respiratory care department are reviewed and audited at least annually 
for competence and qualification to meet the requirements of each position.
