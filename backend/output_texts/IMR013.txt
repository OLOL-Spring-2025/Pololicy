PURPOSE: 
  To outline guidelines for pregnant employees assigned to the MRI area. 
POLICY: 
A. All employees in the MRI area must report their pregnancy to the Supervisor of the area 
as soon as it is known. 
B. The pregnant employee will continue all duties required of their position, with the 
exception of occupying the magnet room during the acquisition of the image. 
C. There currently are no established regulatory guidelines for occupational utilization of 
magnetic resonance imaging systems by pregnant women.
