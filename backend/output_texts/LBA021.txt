LBA021 
 
LABORATORY REFLEX TESTING 
 
 
PURPOSE 
To ensure laboratory reflex testing is approved by the medical staff, meets medical 
necessity guidelines, and is in accordance with Medicare, Medicaid, and other payor 
requirements. 
 
 
DEFINITION 
Reflex Testing- laboratory testing performed subsequent to initial test results, and used to 
further identify significant diagnostic information for appropriate patient care.   
 
 
POLICY 
The Laboratory recognizes that in some instances it is medically appropriate to perform 
reflex testing based on an initial lab result and in other instances reflex testing should be 
at the discretion of the ordering practitioner.  The complete list of Reflex Testing will be 
reviewed and approved by the Medical Executive Committee annually and when 
revisions are made. The following attachment defines Our Lady of the Lake RMC 
Laboratory’s approved Reflex Testing.  These tests will always reflex for confirmation if 
criteria are met indicating the medical necessity for performance of the additional testing.  
All of the following reflex tests will be performed at an additional charge. 
 
The medical staff of OLOLRMC must approve this list of Reflex Tests annually.   
 
 
PROCEDURE 
• Laboratory Director: 
o Identify (with as sistance of lab leadership) all tests that may be reflexed 
when results of the initial test may warrant additional testing. 
o The Medical Director presents recommendations to the Medical Staff for 
approval. 
o Medical Staff approval is documented in the minutes. 
• Lab Leadership (Technical and General Supervisors) 
o Must review and ensure applicable revisions are made to the charge 
master. 
o Laboratory personnel will perform reflex testing only as defined in the 
Medical Executive meeting minutes.
