PURPOSE 
The purpose of this policy is to describe the process to be followed by departments of Our Lady of the 
Lake Regional Medical Center (OLOL) in obtaining services rendered by private employment agencies. 
 
POLICY 
Private employment agencies (i.e., temporary agencies) may be used by individual departments based on 
departmental need as approved by Administration and monitored by Human Resources. 
 
PROCEDURES 
1. Only Administration and designated members of Human Resources will be permitted to place job 
orders on behalf of OLOL.  
 
2. Private employment agencies are to be contacted by Human Resources.   
a. They may have contact with the department head through arrangements made by Human 
Resources.  
b. Human Resources will notify department heads of any such arrangements.   
 
3. If an employment agency should make direct contact with a department, they should be informed of 
OLOL policy and asked to contact Human Resources.
