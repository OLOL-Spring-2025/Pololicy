PURPOSE: 
The purpose of this policy is to define and describe the procedures which must be followed 
to maintain proper and safe operation of the Epic blood gas computer system, and protect 
its components and data. 
 
PROCEDURE: 
Data Backup 
The MIS department has specific guidelines to follow. This information can be obtained 
from this department.  
 
Program Backup 
The MIS department has specific guidelines to follow. This information can be obtained 
from this department. 
 
Data/Program Restoration 
The MIS department has specific guidelines to follow. This information can be obtained 
from this department. 
 
Protection of Equipment 
A. The file server for the EMR is located in the MIS computer room.  Humidity and 
temperature are controlled for equipment protection.  The file server is on the 
hospitalâ€™s Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) to maintain service and protect it 
from power drops and spikes.  The computer room has controlled access. 
B. The PCs servicing each blood gas instrument are each on UPS units to protect them 
and maintain their function. 
 
Maintenance/Cleaning of Equipment Components 
 The file server and its tape drive are cleaned and checked for gross mechanical 
damage or deterioration on the schedule of the other file servers in the MIS 
computer room.  This schedule and the records of maintenance are maintained in 
MIS.
