PURPOSE:  To facilitate communication between the Rehabilitation Nursing staff and the 
Rehabilitation Departments of Physical Therapy, Occupation Therapy, Speech 
Pathology, Case Management / Social Services, and Recreational Therapy, 
allowing for smoother follow-through with patient care. 
 
POLICY:  Written and verbal communication will be done to facilitate communication 
between the Rehab Nursing staff and the Rehab Departments of Physical Therapy, 
Occupational Therapy, Speech Pathology, Case Management / Social Services, 
and Recreational Therapy. 
 
PROCEDURE:  
 
1. A representative from Rehab Nursing will give a verbal SBAR (Situation, Background, 
Assessment, Recommendation) report to a representative from other disciplines every shift. 
2. A F.A.C.T. card will be utilized as a means of written communication.
