PURPOSE: 
Standard precautions are to be taken to prevent employee exposure to infectious organisms 
 
Addresses: 
Venipuncture Charge Techs 
Venipuncture Phlebotomists 
Venipuncture Supervisors 
 
POLICY: 
All patients and samples are to be treated as though infectious.  Precautions are to be taken regardless of patient 
diagnosis or presumed infectious state.  Specimens from known infectious patients are not labeled differently than 
any other patient sample. 
A. Venipuncture personnel are to wear gloves while: 
1. Performing Venipuncture and capillary blood collection 
2. Collecting outpatient urine specimens or other patient specimens 
3. Processing and logging in samples into the laboratory 
B. Gloves are to be removed and discarded after each patient phlebotomy or specimen collection 
C. Gloves worn while processing specimens in the lab should be changed hourly, if torn, or visibly soiled with 
blood or other body material 
D. Do not open specimen containers without wearing gloves 
E. Hands are to be washed following glove removal 
F. Do not wash, disinfect, or reuse surgical or examination gloves
